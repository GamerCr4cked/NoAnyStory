Pehli Nazar

10th class ka wahi boring sa routine — subah school, class me bakchodi, doston ke saath timepass aur phir ghar. Zindagi me kuch khaas nahi chal raha tha. Main bhi ek average ladka — na koi special talent, na koi interesting kahani. Bas ek ordinary sa insaan.

Ek din school khatam hone ke baad ka scene tha. Mere ek dost ka crush usi raste se ghar jaati thi, toh hum sab usko dekhne ke bahaane wahi khade the. Koi bada plan nahi — bas dekhna aur thoda mazaak udana.

Kuch din baad ek dopahar main shortcut wale raste se ghar ja raha tha — thoda lamba padta hai par bheed kam hoti hai. Saamne se ek ladki aa rahi thi. Mask lagaya hua tha usne, par is baar maine usse seedha dekh liya.

Aur pata nahi kyu, andar kuch hila. Koi electric shock jaisa nahi — bas ek halka sa tug, jaise kuch kehna chahta ho par shabd nahi mil rahe. Ek second ke liye pair thame, phir dimaag ne bola "chal aage badh" aur main nikal gaya.

Socha normal hai. Koi baat nahi. Main toh waise hi hu.

Par kuch cheezein normal nahi hoti — wo bas normal dikhti hain.

Assembly ka din tha. Subah ki dhoop, lines me khade bacche, teachers ka usual lecture. Main apni line me tha, half-asleep. Tabhi gate khula aur ek ladki andar aayi — late thi, thoda jaldi jaldi chal rahi thi. Seedha girls ki line me jaake khadi ho gayi.

Aur is baar… mask nahi tha.

Pehli baar uska chehra properly dikha. Itni simple thi. Na koi fancy style, na koi attention seek karne wala attitude. Bas ek clean, honest sa face. Aur pata nahi kaise — us bheed me, us shor me — meri nazar sirf usi pe ruk gayi.

Us din ke baad ek naya pattern shuru ho gaya. Mera dhyan baar baar wahin jaata tha — assembly me, corridor me, gate ke paas. Bina reason. Bina plan ke.

Dheere dheere pata chala — wo 7th class ki hai. Aur main 10th ka. Teen saal ka gap. Logically thoda ajeeb lagna chahiye tha… par feelings logic se kab chali hain.

Uska naam nahi pata tha. Class nahi pata thi. Kuch nahi pata tha. Par phir bhi us ladki ke baare me sochna band nahi ho raha tha. Toh maine apne mann me usko ek naam de diya —
