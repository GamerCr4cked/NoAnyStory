Ajnabi

Arhan kabhi kisi se pehle baat nahi karta tha.

Introvert nahi tha — bas ye believe tha ki agar kisi ko baat karni hogi, toh wo karegi. Usse force karne ki zaroorat nahi. Ye philosophy 18 saal tak acche se kaam ki thi. College me bhi — pehle din, ek corner seat pakdi, earphones in, duniya out.

BSc first year. New city. New hostel. Sab naya. Par Arhan ke liye "naya" ka matlab tha "ignore karo jab tak comfortable na ho." Aur comfortable hone me usse weeks lag jaate the.

Par kuch log hote hain jo tumhari poori philosophy ek din me tod dete hain.

Orientation day tha. Auditorium packed. AC kaam nahi kar raha tha. Har koi uncomfortable tha. Arhan second row se last seat pe tha — strategically chosen taaki koi uske paas na baithe.

Par fate ko strategy nahi samajh aata.

Ek ladki aayi. Late. Thodi bhaagi hui. Dupatta sambhalte hue, bag ek shoulder pe, haath me ek cold coffee ka glass jo already half-spill ho chuka tha. Poori row dekhi — sab full. Sirf ek seat khaali — Arhan ke bagal me.

Inaya

khaali hai
Inaya

Ye bolke usne bag neeche rakha, coffee ka glass carefully side me rakhaa, aur baith gayi. Arhan ne earphone wapas lagane ki koshish ki.

Inaya

Arhan ko hansi aa gayi. Bina chahe. Bina plan kiye.

Ye pehli baar tha ki kisi ajnabi ne usse pehle 2 minute me hasa diya.

Arhan normally mana kar deta. Hamesha karta tha. "Nahi, mujhe kaam hai" ya "aaj nahi" bolke nikal jaata. Par aaj —

Arhan

Khud ko surprise hua. Par zyada sochne se pehle wo dono canteen ki taraf chal rahe the.

Kuch rishte permission nahi maangte. Wo bas shuru ho jaate hain — aur tumhe baad me pata chalta hai.

Aadat

Pehle hafte Arhan ne socha — ye temporary hai. College me log pehle din baat karte hain, phir apne groups bana lete hain, aur puraane connections fade ho jaate hain. Inaya bhi aisa hi hogi — ek din milegi, phir apni duniya me busy ho jayegi.

Par wo galat tha.

Dusre din lecture me Inaya ne poora auditorium scan kiya — aur seedha uski taraf aayi.

Inaya
kal wali seat. same. chalo baitho.

Teesre din bhi. Chauthe din bhi. Hafte ke end tak — wo seat officially "unki" ho gayi thi. Koi aur baithta toh Inaya usse look deti jaise usne crime kiya ho.

Second week me canteen trip bhi daily ban gayi. Arhan black coffee peeta tha — boring, simple. Inaya har baar kuch naya try karti — cold coffee, smoothie, milkshake, ek din toh green tea bhi mangaayi (2 sip ke baad chhod di).

Arhan
ek cheez pe tik nahi sakti?
Inaya
boring logo ki nishani hai consistency. main adventurous hu.
Arhan
green tea 2 sip me chhod dena adventurous nahi, paisa barbad karna hai
Inaya

Arhan

Inaya
education free nahi hoti, Arhan

Har baat me logic. Galat logic — par confidence se bola jaaye toh sab sahi lagta hai.

Ek mahine me Arhan ko realize hua — uski puri routine Inaya ke around restructure ho gayi thi.

Subah ka pehla text uska hota tha — "utho. late ho rahe ho." (Ironic, kyuki wo khud hamesha late hoti thi.) Lectures me saath baithte. Lunch saath me hota. Shaam ko library ya canteen. Aur raat ko phone pe — kabhi calls, kabhi texts, kabhi bas memes.

Ek din uska roommate Faraz bola:

"Bhai, tu Inaya ke bina ek din bhi guzaar sakta hai kya?"

Arhan hasa. Bola "obviously. Wo sirf friend hai."

Par raat ko sochte waqt realize hua — usse yaad nahi tha last time kab usse bina baat kiye din guzra ho.

Aadat. Ye aadat thi. Par kab "aadat" ban gayi — ye usse pata nahi chala.

Best Friends

Teen mahine. Ek semester almost khatam. Aur ab "Arhan aur Inaya" ek package deal ban chuke the. Koi ek ko dhundhe toh dusra paas hi milta.

College me sab jaante the — ye dono best friends hain. Kuch log sochte the zyada hai, par dono deny karte the itni confidently ki logon ne maan liya.

Inaya ke baare me bohot kuch pata chal gaya in teen mahinon me:

  • Wo chutkulon pe nahi, situations pe hasti thi — kabhi itna zor se ki poora canteen muri
  • Usse baarish bohot pasand thi — ek baar Arhan ko kheench ke baarish me le gayi, dono bheeg gaye, dono beemar hue
  • Wo raat ko 2 baje tak jaag ke chhoti chhoti poems likhti thi — kabhi kisi ko nahi dikhayi. Sirf Arhan ko. "Par batana kisi ko nahi"
  • Usse dark chocolate pasand thi — "milk chocolate bacchon ke liye hai"
  • Aur usse bohot darr lagta tha akele rehne se. Par ye baat usne kabhi directly nahi boli — Arhan ne khud notice kiya

Arhan ke baare me bhi usne sab seekh liya:

  • Wo feelings express karne me terrible tha — par actions se sab karta tha
  • Jab wo serious hota tha toh aankhein thodi narrow hoti thi — Inaya bina bole samajh jaati thi kab kuch galat hai
  • Usse photography ka shauk tha — par kabhi post nahi karta tha. Sirf click karta tha aur gallery me rakh deta tha
  • Wo apne ghar ki baatein kabhi nahi karta tha — Inaya ne pucha bhi nahi kabhi. Bas ek din bola dega ye pata tha
  • Aur jab wo nervous hota tha — apni watch ka strap ghoomata rehta tha

Ek raat — exam ke stress me — Inaya ka call aaya 1 AM pe. Arhan turant utha.

Arhan
kya hua?

Wo ro rahi thi. Chup chaap. Wo type ki crying jisme shor nahi hota — bas saans bhaari hoti hai aur beech beech me ek hiccup aata hai.

Arhan

Arhan

Inaya

Arhan

Boys hostel aur girls hostel ke beech ek common garden tha. Technically raat ko jaana allowed nahi tha. Par Arhan ko rules ki parwaah nahi thi — us waqt nahi.

10 minute me dono garden ki bench pe the. December ki raat. Thandi hawa. Uski aankhein laal thi par ab ro nahi rahi thi.

Arhan ne kuch nahi pucha. Kuch nahi bola. Bas wahi baitha raha. 20 minute. Silence me.

Phir Inaya ne apna sar uske shoulder pe rakh diya. Dheere se. Jaise permission le rahi ho bina bolke.

Arhan hila nahi. Bas baitha raha. Uski saans feel karta raha.

Inaya
thanks
Arhan
kisliye?
Inaya
aane ke liye. bina puche.
Arhan
hamesha aaunga. pucho ya na pucho.

Wo chup rahi. Par uski grip uski dupatte pe thodi tight hui. Jaise wo sentence usse andar tak pahuncha ho.

Best friends. Yahi the. Yahi the na?

Kuch Alag

Second semester shuru hua aur kuch slowly shift hone laga. Wo changes itne chhote the ki dono ne ignore kiya — par dono feel kar rahe the.

Pehle — Arhan ne notice kiya ki wo uske liye zyada effort daal raha hai. Canteen me pehle wo apni coffee order karta aur usse bolna hota "khud order karo." Ab wo automatically uska bhi order de deta tha — bina puche, kyuki usse pata tha kya chahiye. Aaj cold coffee mood hai ya hot chocolate. Bina puche.

Inaya ne notice kiya ki wo uske saath alag behave karti hai. Apni doston ke saath loud thi, crazy thi. Par Arhan ke saath — thodi soft ho jaati thi. Thodi calm. Jaise uske aas paas ek safe space exist karta ho jaha pretend nahi karna padta.

Ek din library me dono padh rahe the. Arhan focus me tha. Inaya bore ho gayi thi (usual). Usne apna pen Arhan ke notebook pe rakh diya — seedha uski writing ke upar.

Arhan

Usne uski notebook kheench li. Arhan ne pakadne ki koshish ki. Dono ke haath ek saath notebook pe the — aur ek second ke liye unki ungliyan touch ho gayi.

Dono ruk gaye.

Ek second. Do second.

Inaya ne haath kheencha. Notebook wapas di. "Lo. padho." Phir apna phone uthaya aur screen dekhne lagi — par scroll nahi kar rahi thi. Bas dekh rahi thi.

Arhan ne apni notebook uthayi. Par us din ke baad — us page pe kuch nahi likh paaya. Har baar aankh wahi jaati thi jaha uski ungliyan thi.

Ek shaam campus me walk kar rahe the. Sunset tha — golden light poore ground pe. Inaya ne phone nikala photo lene ke liye.

Kabhi kabhi pyaar ek photo lene se shuru hota hai. Sunset ki nahi — us insaan ki jise tum sunset se zyada sundar samajhte ho.

Ab raat ki calls lamba hone lagi. 11 se shuru ho jaati aur 2 tak chalti. Topics random — par beech beech me kuch moments aate jab dono chup ho jaate. Wo comfortable chuppi nahi thi — wo loaded thi. Jisme dono ke andar kuch tha jo bahar aana chahta tha par rasta nahi mil raha tha.

Lambi chuppi. Phone pe ek dusre ki saans sunaayi de rahi thi.

Inaya
mujhe bhi nahi lagta.

Bol Do

Us raat ke baad — kuch officially nahi badla. Par sab kuch unofficially badal gaya.

Ab nazrein thodi aur zyada tikti thi. Haath thoda aur paas rehte the. Aur wo loaded silence — wo ab zyada frequently aane laga. Jaise dono ke andar ek timer chal raha ho jo kisi na kisi din bajega.

Arhan ke andar ek civil war chal rahi thi.

Ek taraf — "bol de. Jo feel kar raha hai bol de. Worst case kya hoga? Mana kar degi? At least clear ho jayega."

Dusri taraf — "agar bol diya aur usne same feel nahi kiya toh? Friendship khatam. Wo awkward ho jayega. Aur tum phir se akele."

Dusri taraf hamesha jeet jaati thi. Kyuki Arhan ke liye Inaya ko khona — wo possibility itni terrifying thi ki risk lena impossible lagta tha.

Par fate ne decide kiya ki wait aur nahi hoga.

College fest tha. February. Poora campus decorated. Music, stalls, crowd. Arhan normally festivals avoid karta tha — par Inaya ne literally kheench ke le gayi.

Inaya
ek baar zindagi me fun karo please. tumhare liye request hai ye.

Poora din saath ghumte rahe. Stalls dekhe, khaana khaaya, ek photography competition me Arhan ne participate kiya (Inaya ne force kiya — aur wo second aaya). Shaam ko live music tha — campus ke open-air amphitheater me.

Dono last row me baithe the. Aasmaan dark ho raha tha. Stage pe koi acoustic gaana gaa raha tha. Hawa thodi thandi thi.

Inaya ne apne haath apni goud me rakhe the. Thand ki wajah se ungliyan thodi laal thi.

Arhan ne bina soche uska haath pakad liya.

Inaya ruki. Uski taraf dekha. Par kuch boli nahi. Aur haath bhi nahi kheencha.

2 minute. 5 minute. Gaana khatam hua. Dusra shuru hua. Log taaliyaan baja rahe the. Par un dono ki duniya us bench pe thi — haath me haath, bina kisi ne kuch bole.

Garden ke paas pahunche — wahi garden jaha ek raat Inaya royi thi aur Arhan chup chaap baitha tha. Arhan ruk gaya.

Tum mere liye bohot important ho. Itni ki darr lagta hai — ki agar ye bol diya toh sab kharab ho jayega. Par nahi bolunga toh andar se kharab ho raha hu.

Sab ek saans me bola. Bina ruke. Bina soche. Jaise ek dam toota ho aur saara paani ek saath beh gaya.

Dono ke chehre pe smile aayi — ek saath. Waisi wali jo relief se aati hai, khushi se aati hai, aur thodi si "finally" se aati hai.

Kuch confessions rehearse nahi hote. Wo bas bahar aa jaate hain — raw, shaky, imperfect. Par wahi sabse sacche hote hain.

Humara Wala Pyaar

Uske baad ki duniya alag thi. Wahi college, wahi canteen, wahi bench, wahi log — par sab kuch naya lag raha tha. Jaise kisi ne duniya pe ek naya filter laga diya ho — warmer, brighter, softer.

Relationship announce nahi ki. Kisi ko formally nahi bataya. Par sab samajh gaye the — jaise hamesha hota hai. Faraz ne ek din Arhan se bola:

"Bhai, tere chehre se pata chal jaata hai. Smile zyada ho gayi hai. Pehle robot lagta tha, ab insaan lagta hai."

Inaya ki friend Zara ne bhi kuch similar bola:

"Tu aaj kal zyada khush dikh rahi hai. Arhan ka asar hai ya vitamin D ka?"

Inaya hasi: "Dono."

Pyaar unka kuch alag tha. Na filmy, na cringe. Bas… real.

Ek din barish ho rahi thi. Dono canteen me the. Inaya window ke paas baithi thi, barish dekh rahi thi. Arhan apni coffee pee raha tha.

Inaya

"Tumhare saath." Ye do shabd kaafi the.

5 minute baad dono baarish me the. Ground pe. Bheeg rahe the. Inaya haath failaake ghoom rahi thi jaise chhoti bacchi ho. Arhan khada tha — thoda bheegte hue, thoda muskurate hue, aur usse dekh raha tha.

Phir wo uske paas aayi. Bheegi hui. Baal chehre pe chipke hue. Aankhon me wo wali spark.

Inaya

Usne uska collar pakda — halka sa — aur seedha aankhon me dekha. Barish dono pe gir rahi thi. Duniya blur thi. Sirf wo do clear the.

Inaya

Usne apna maatha uske mathe se laga diya. Barish me. Campus ke beech me. Duniya dekhti rahi — par un dono ko farak nahi pada.

Ye unka moment tha. Raw. Wet. Imperfect. Par bilkul unjaisa — beautiful.

First year khatam hone ko aaya. Exams aaye, gaye. Results aaye — dono pass. Summer break shuru hone wala tha — matlab alag shaharon me jaana. Pehla proper separation.

Phir Milenge.

Ye do shabd — jo usne casually bole the — baad me us poori kahani ka naam ban jayenge. Par us waqt ye sirf ek promise tha. Ek saccha, simple promise.

Do logo ka. Jo sochte the ye hamesha rahega.

Phir Milenge — Season 1

Ajnabi se best friend. Best friend se pyaar. Aur pyaar me wo golden days jinhein hum hamesha yaad rakhte hain — kyuki jo aage aane wala hai… usse pehle ye jaanna zaroori hai ki ye kitna khoobsurat tha.
