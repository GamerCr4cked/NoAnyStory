Pehli Nazar

10th class ka ek normal phase chal raha tha — class, bakchodi, doston ke saath timepass. Main bhi wahi, close log ke liye UNUN, doston me GC, aur baaki sab ke liye GamerCracked.

Ek din school ke baad hum sab ek dost ke crush ke liye wait kar rahe the. Plan simple tha — usko dekhna aur thoda mazaak.

Wo jis raste se jaati thi hum waha khade the, par ek aur rasta bhi tha. Door se ek ladki ko aate dekha, bag same type ka laga, humne socha wahi hai aur bina confirm kiye nikal liye. Next day jab paas gaye tab pata chala — wo to wo thi hi nahi. Mask pehni hui thi kal, aur humne kisi aur ko hi samajh liya tha. Baat wahi khatam.

Kuch din baad main shortcut wale raste se ja raha tha, tabhi saamne se ek ladki aa rahi thi. Wo wahi thi.

Mask pehna hua tha, par is baar maine usko seedha samne se dekha… aur pata nahi kyu andar kuch alag sa feel hua. Ek second ke liye laga ruk jaun, par phir ignore karke nikal gaya.

Socha normal hai… main to waise hi hu.

Phir aaya wo din jo sab change kar gaya.

Assembly chal rahi thi. Main line me khada tha. Wo late aayi… gate se enter karke seedha girls line me khadi ho gayi.

Is baar mask nahi tha.

Aur bas… wahi moment tha.

Usko dekhte hi laga jaise sab slow ho gaya ho. Itni simple… itni clean… itni alag.

Us din ke baad pata nahi kaise, par mera dhyaan baar baar usi taraf jaane laga.

Din beetne lage.

Bina baat kiye, bina kuch jaane… bas door se dekhte dekhte main uske pyaar me padta gaya.

Phir pata chala — wo 7th class ki hai. Aur main 10th ka.

Thoda ajeeb lagna chahiye tha… par nahi laga.
