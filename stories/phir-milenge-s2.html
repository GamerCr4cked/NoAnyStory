Distance

Summer break shuru hua. Arhan apne ghar gaya — chhota sa sheher, chhoti si duniya. Inaya apne ghar — alag sheher, alag duniya. Beech me 800 km ki distance aur ek phone screen.

Pehla hafta accha gaya. Roz call — subah, dopahar, raat. Memes, voice notes, "miss kar rahi hu" texts. Sab normal. Sab khoobsurat.

Dusra hafta — thoda slow hua. Inaya busy rehne lagi. Calls chhoti hone lagi. "Ghar pe kaam hai" ya "relatives aaye hain" ya "thak gayi hu." Valid reasons. Arhan ne kuch nahi socha.

Teesra hafta — replies late aane lage. Arhan subah text karta, reply dopahar ko aata. Call karta toh "abhi busy hu, baad me?" Baad me kabhi kabhi aata, kabhi nahi.

Arhan
sab theek hai?
Inaya
haan. bas family drama. kuch nahi.

Arhan ne maan liya. Kyuki trust tha. Poora trust. Wo type ka trust jisme tum kisi ki baat bina verify kiye maan lete ho — kyuki tumhe lagta hai ye insaan jhooth nahi bolega. Kabhi nahi.

Fourth week me Arhan ne uski Instagram story dekhi. Ek group photo — Inaya, kuch ladkiyan jo uski school friends thi, aur ek ladka. Wo ladka paas me khada tha — Inaya ke paas. Thoda zyada paas.

Normal photo thi. Group outing. Kuch bhi wrong nahi tha. Par Arhan ke andar kuch hila — wo type ka hila jo logical nahi hota par gut feel hota hai.

Usne pucha casually:

Arhan
story dekhi. kaun tha wo ladka?
Inaya
Rehan. school friend hai. purane group ka hissa hai.
Arhan
pehle kabhi naam nahi suna
Inaya
bohot saare friends hain jinke naam nahi sune honge. chill karo.

"Chill karo." Ye do shabd — Arhan ko pehli baar usse sunke acche nahi lage. Jaise dismiss kiya gaya ho.

Par usne ignore kiya. Kyuki trust. Kyuki pyaar. Kyuki "main overthink kar raha hu."

Shadows

Second year shuru hua. Dono wapas college. Pehle din Arhan ne Inaya ko dekha hostel ke bahar — aur sab theek laga. Wahi smile. Wahi energy. Usne hug kiya — tight, lamba. Arhan ne saans li. Sab normal hai. Summer ka distance sirf distance tha. Ab sab theek hoga.

Par kuch chhoti cheezein different thi.

Inaya ka phone zyada busy rehne laga. Pehle wo phone table pe rakh deti thi — screen up, notifications visible. Ab phone hamesha ulta hota tha. Ya pocket me.

Pehle wo messages aate hi reply karti thi — ab kabhi kabhi 30 minute, kabhi ek ghanta. "Sorry, class me thi" ya "phone silent pe tha."

Pehle wo Arhan ko sab batati thi — har chhoti baat, har pal. Ab kuch gaps aane lage. "Kal kya kiya?" ka jawab "kuch nahi, usual" hone laga. Pehle "usual" me bhi details hoti thi. Ab sirf ek shabd.

Arhan ne notice kiya. Sab notice kiya. Par bola nahi — kyuki pichli baar jab pucha tha "sab theek hai?" toh "chill karo" mila tha. Aur wo phir se wo insaan nahi banna chahta tha jo overthink karta hai.

Par overthink karna aur observe karna me farq hota hai. Aur Arhan observe kar raha tha.

Ek din canteen me dono baithe the. Inaya ka phone baja. Usne screen dekha — aur turant utha ke bahar chali gayi. "Ek minute, call leni hai."

10 minute baad wapas aayi. Muskura rahi thi — par wo natural smile nahi thi. Wo "cover up" wali thi.

Arhan
kiska call tha?
Inaya
ghar se tha.

Arhan ne neeche dekha uske phone pe — notification bar me ek naam tha. "Rehan."

Ghar se nahi tha.

Arhan ne kuch nahi bola. Phone ka screen band ho gaya. Moment guzar gaya. Par Arhan ke andar kuch tootne laga — dheere dheere, bina awaaz ke.

Third Person

Rehan ka naam ab zyada aane laga — indirectly.

Inaya kabhi kabhi kisi ke saath phone pe hasti rehti thi raat ko — jab Arhan call karta toh "busy hu" bolti. Uski Instagram pe comments aane lage — ek specific account se. Heart emojis. "Looking great." Casual? Shayad. Par frequency casual nahi thi.

Arhan ne ek din directly pucha:

Arhan
ye Rehan kaun hai exactly? School friend toh bola tha. Par school friend itna active nahi hota usually.
Inaya
Arhan please. possessive mat ho. wo sirf friend hai. purana dost hai. reconnect ho gaya summer me.
Arhan
possessive nahi hu. par jab tum mujhse chhupati ho toh mujhe lagta hai kuch galat hai.
Inaya
main kuch nahi chhupa rahi. tum overthink kar rahe ho. hamesha ki tarah.

"Hamesha ki tarah." Ye hurt kiya. Jaise uski concern ek character flaw hai.

Arhan
theek hai. sorry.

Usne sorry bol diya. Apni galti nahi hone pe bhi. Kyuki fight nahi chahta tha. Kyuki Inaya ko khona nahi chahta tha. Kyuki pyaar me log sorry bolte hain — chahe galti na ho — sirf isliye ki rishta bachana zyada zaroori lagta hai apne aap se.

Par sach chhupata nahi — bas late aata hai.

Ek raat Arhan library se late aa raha tha. Hostel ke paas garden ke raaste se gaya — wahi garden. Aur wahi bench pe — jaha ek raat Inaya royi thi aur usne chup chaap saath diya tha — waha Inaya baithi thi.

Rehan ke saath.

Dono has rahe the. Inaya ka sar Rehan ke shoulder pe tha — waise hi, casually, jaise Arhan ke shoulder pe rakhti thi.

Arhan ruk gaya. Andhera tha. Wo nahi dikh raha tha unhe. Par wo unhe dekh sakta tha — clearly.

Uska poora sharir sann ho gaya. Pair jud gaye. Saans ruk gayi. Aankhen — wo dekhna nahi chahta tha par hatt nahi rahi thi.

Phir Rehan ne Inaya ka haath pakda. Aur Inaya ne —

Hata nahi.

Arhan wahi khada raha. 30 seconds. Shayad ek minute. Phir dheere se peeche muda aur chala gaya. Bina awaaz ke. Bina kisi ko pata chale.

Hostel aaya. Room me gaya. Darwaza band kiya. Bed pe baitha.

Aur poori raat ceiling dekhta raha. Bina ek aansoo ke. Bina ek shabd ke.

Kyuki jab sab se zyada dard hota hai — tab rona nahi aata. Tab sirf khamoshi aati hai. Wo type ki khamoshi jo andar se khaati hai.

Collapse

Agle din Arhan normally mila Inaya se. Class me same seat. Canteen me same jagah. Par kuch alag tha — uski aankhon me wo warmth gayab thi. Wo wala look jo kehta tha "tum meri duniya ho." Ab bas ek neutral expression tha. Polite. Distant.

Inaya ne notice kiya — 2 din baad.

Inaya
kya hua? kuch alag ho tum aaj kal.
Arhan
kuch nahi. thak gaya hu. exams ki tension.

Wahi excuse. Wahi jhooth. Par is baar Arhan de raha tha — Inaya nahi.

Ek hafta aur guzra. Arhan chup rehne laga. Calls kam kar di. Messages chhote hone lage. Wahi pattern jo Inaya ne summer me kiya tha — ab Arhan kar raha tha. Par wajah alag thi. Inaya ki wajah confusion thi. Arhan ki wajah — dard.

Ek raat Inaya ne call kiya. Late — 12 baje.

Inaya
Arhan. kya ho raha hai? seedha bolo.

Arhan ne phone pakda. Ceiling dekhi. Deep breath liya.

Arhan
maine tumhe garden me dekha tha. Rehan ke saath.

Silence. 5 seconds. 10 seconds.

Inaya
Arhan wo kuch nahi —
Arhan
uska haath pakda hua tha. tumhara sar uske shoulder pe tha. wahi bench pe. humari bench pe.

Silence phir se. Lambi. Heavy. Wo type ki jisme tum dono jaante ho ki ab kuch bhi bolo — sab badal chuka hai.

Inaya
mujhe explain karne do —
Arhan
kya explain karogi? ki wo "sirf friend" hai? ki "main overthink kar raha hu"? ki "chill karo"?

Uski awaaz nahi kaanp rahi thi. Na gussa tha, na cheekhna. Bas ek thanda, steady tone — wo type ka tone jo gusse se zyada dangerous hota hai. Kyuki gussa guzar jaata hai. Ye nahi guzarta.

Inaya
Arhan please suno —
Arhan
sun raha hu. bolo.
Inaya
wo… Rehan… hum summer me reconnect hue the. Aur haan, wo mujhe pasand karta hai. Aur main — main confused hu. Maine galat kiya. Main jaanti hu. Par main tumse pyaar karti hu Arhan. Sachchi me.
Arhan
pyaar karti ho par kisi aur ka haath pakdti ho humari bench pe?
Inaya
wo ek moment tha. Galti thi. Main —
Arhan
tumne mujhse jhooth bola. Baar baar. "Ghar se call tha." "Sirf friend hai." "Overthink mat karo." Har baar jhooth. Aur main — main har baar maan gaya. Kyuki mujhe tumpe trust tha.

Uski awaaz ek second ke liye crack hui — "trust tha" bolte waqt. Par usne sambhaal liya.

Inaya
Arhan main sorry hu. Bohot sorry. Please —
Arhan
sorry se trust wapas nahi aata, Inaya.

Lambi silence. Phone pe dono ki saans sunaayi de rahi thi. Inaya ro rahi thi — Arhan sun sakta tha. Par is baar wo garden me nahi jayega. Is baar wo chup chaap nahi baithega.

Arhan
mujhe waqt chahiye. Please mujhse door raho kuch din.
Inaya
Arhan mat karo ye. please. main theek kar dungi sab —
Arhan
goodnight, Inaya.

Call end ki. Phone neeche rakha. Haath kaamp rahe the.

Aur tab — pehli baar — aansoo aaye. Chup chaap. Bina awaaz ke. Pillow pe face rakhke. Itna roya ki neend aa gayi.

Trust tootna — ye pyaar tootne se bhi zyada dard deta hai. Kyuki pyaar wapas aa sakta hai. Par trust? Wo ek baar gaya toh wapas laana — duniya ka sabse mushkil kaam hai.

Aftermath

Agle din se Arhan ne sab band kar diya. Calls nahi uthaye. Messages padhe par reply nahi kiya. Canteen me alag baitha. Class me seat change kar li.

Inaya ne try kiya — bohot try kiya. Messages bheje. Long paragraphs. Voice notes jisme ro rahi thi. Garden me wait ki — par wo nahi aaya. Common friends ke through message bheja — Arhan ne bola "mujhe waqt chahiye."

Vikram ne ek raat room me baith ke pucha:

Vikram
bhai, kya hua? Inaya ro rahi hai roz. tu baat nahi kar raha. bata toh sahi.
Arhan
usne kisi aur ke saath — humari jagah pe — mera bharosa toda. aur phir mujhe bola "overthink mat karo." baar baar. main pagal samjha khud ko.
Vikram
…bhai.

Vikram chup ho gaya. Kuch nahi bola. Kyuki kuch kehne ko tha hi nahi. Bas Arhan ke paas baith gaya — wahi silence de di jo kabhi kabhi shabdon se zyada kaam karti hai.

Arhan ke andar kuch badal gaya tha. Wo pehle wala Arhan nahi raha — jo dheere dheere open hua tha, jo muskurane laga tha, jo kisi ke liye raat ko 1 baje garden me jaata tha.

Wo phir se band ho gaya. Earphones in. Duniya out. Wahi purana pattern. Par is baar — ye pattern choice nahi tha. Ye survival tha.

Class jaata. Khaana khaata. Padhayi karta. Sab kuch mechanically — jaise ek robot jo programmed hai par feel nahi karta.

Inaya ki taraf — sab toot raha tha.

Meera
Inaya… tu theek hai?
Inaya
nahi Meera. main theek nahi hu. maine sab barbaad kar diya.
Meera
galti hui hai. par tu theek kar sakti hai. pehle Rehan ko block kar. phir Arhan ko waqt de.
Inaya
aur agar wo wapas nahi aaya toh?
Meera
toh bhi — pehle tu khud ko theek kar. baaki baad me dekhna.

Usne Rehan se baat karna band kar diya — turant, poora. "Maine galat kiya. Please ab contact mat karo." Rehan ne argue kiya, par usne block kar diya. Par ye sab karne se Arhan wapas nahi aaya.

Wo roz usse dekhti thi campus me — door se. Wahi Arhan, wahi walk, wahi earphones. Par ab wo uski taraf dekhta nahi tha. Jaise wo exist hi nahi karti.

Invisibility ka dard — jab wo insaan tumhe ignore kare jiske liye tum duniya thi — ye kisi bhi saza se buri hai.

Ek mahina guzra. Phir do. Second year ka first semester khatam hone ko aaya.

Ek din Arhan library me tha. Padhte padhte uski nazar window se bahar gayi — ground pe Inaya apni friends ke saath thi. Has rahi thi. Normal lag rahi thi.

Par Arhan jaanta tha — wo wali hansi asli nahi thi. Kyuki usne wo asli hansi dekhi thi, suni thi, feel ki thi. Aur ye wo nahi thi.

Ek second ke liye uska dil kheencha — jaise koi purani aadat jaag gayi ho. "Ja. Baat kar. Maaf kar de."

Par phir wo bench ki image aayi. Uska sar Rehan ke shoulder pe. Uska haath uske haath me. Wahi bench.

Arhan ne window se nazar hatayi. Kitaab kholi. Aur padhayi me ghus gaya.

Khamoshi

Semester khatam hua. Winter break aaya. Dono apne apne ghar gaye — alag shaharon me. 800 km ki distance. Par is baar wo distance pyaar ki nahi, khamoshi ki thi.

Na koi call. Na koi text. Na koi story reply. Kuch nahi.

Arhan ke din ek jaisi the. Subah uthna. Chai. Padhayi. Khaana. Sona. Repeat. Uski gallery me abhi bhi Inaya ki photos thi — delete nahi ki thi. Par dekhta bhi nahi tha.

Kabhi kabhi raat ko 2 baje aankh khulti — aur pehla khayal hota "Inaya ko message karu." Phir yaad aata. Aur aankh band karke phir so jaata.

Ek din purani chats scroll kar raha tha — bina socha accidentally uska chat khul gaya. Pehle messages dikhe — orientation day, canteen, green tea wala joke. Aur phir baarish wala din. "Main tumse bohot pyaar karti hu. Pata hai na?"

Phone lock kiya. Neeche rakha. Deep breath.

Yaadein chaku nahi hoti — wo aag hoti hain. Dheemi, slow burn. Koi wound nahi dikhta — par andar sab jala deti hain.

Inaya ke ghar pe bhi sab wahi tha. Surface pe normal — par andar hollow.

Uski mummy ne ek din pucha: "Kya hua? Hasna band kyu kar diya?"

Usne bola: "Kuch nahi. Bas tired hu."

Par raat ko — jab sab so jaate — wo apna phone uthati, Arhan ka number dekhti, aur type karti:

"I'm sorry. Main jaanti hu sorry kaafi nahi hai. Par main tumhe batana chahti hu ki maine sabse bada galti ki apni zindagi me. Aur main har din regret karti hu."

Type karti. Padhti. Delete karti. Har raat. Same routine.

Break khatam hua. College wapas. Second year ka last semester.

Dono wapas aaye. Same campus. Same buildings. Par ab dono alag duniya me the.

Arhan ne ek naya routine bana liya — jisme Inaya nahi thi. Nayi friends nahi banayi — par purane logon ke saath thoda zyada rehne laga. Library zyada jaane laga. Photography seriously lene laga — campus ki photos Instagram pe daalne laga. Logon ko naya Arhan dikha — thoda quiet, thoda serious, par stable.

Inaya bhi apni life me adjust kar rahi thi. Zara ke saath zyada rehne lagi. Padhayi pe focus kiya. Par har corridor me, har canteen ki line me, har bench ke paas — Arhan ki yaad thi.

Ek din campus me dono aamne saamne aa gaye. Corridor me. Avoid karne ka chance nahi tha.

Dono ruke. Ek second ki eye contact.

Arhan ne halka sa nod kiya — polite, distant. Jaise kisi acquaintance ko karte hain.

Inaya ne bhi nod kiya. Uski aankhon me bohot kuch tha — sorry, regret, pyaar, dard — par zubaan pe kuch nahi aaya.

Dono aage badh gaye. Alag directions me.

Kabhi sochte ho — do log jo roz ek dusre ke bina nahi reh sakte the, ek din ek dusre ke paas se guzar jaate hain jaise ajnabi hon? Wo moment — wo exact moment — wo sabse zyada toothta hai.

Second year khatam hua. Summer break phir aaya.

Is baar na koi call ki, na koi message. 800 km ki distance — par is baar feel bhi nahi hua. Kyuki dono already itna door the ki physical distance ka koi matlab nahi raha.

Arhan ne ek raat apni notebook kholi — wahi jisme college ke pehle din likhna shuru kiya tha. Last entry 6 mahine pehle ki thi. Usne pen uthaya aur likha:

"Kuch log tumhari zindagi me aate hain tumhe khoobsurat cheezein dikhane ke liye. Phir wahi log tumhe wo dard dikhate hain jo tumne socha nahi tha ki exist karta hai. Aur phir — wo chale jaate hain. Dono cheezein leke. Khoobsurati bhi. Dard bhi. Aur tumhare paas sirf khamoshi bachti hai."

Phir Milenge — Season 2

Pyaar tootha. Trust tootha. Aur wo do insaan jo ek dusre ke bina nahi reh sakte the — ab ek dusre ke bina rehna seekh rahe hain.


Par kahani abhi khatam nahi hui.

Kyuki usne bola tha — "phir milenge."
