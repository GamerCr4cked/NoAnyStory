Wrong Flat

Aarav ke paas do suitcase the, ek backpack tha, aur zindagi me bilkul koi plan nahi tha.

Freelancer tha — graphic design, logo, kabhi kabhi kisi startup ka pura branding package. Kaam aata tha, chala jaata tha. Stable kuch nahi tha. Par Delhi me ek kamra chahiye tha — bas itna kaafi tha naya shuru karne ke liye.

Broker ka naam Pandey ji tha. Chhota aadmi, bada phone, aur har baat me "tension mat lo sir" bolne ki aadat.

Aarav
Pandey ji, flat kaisa hai? Zyada mahanga toh nahi na?
Pandey ji
Sir tension mat lo. 2BHK hai, furnished hai, aur rent itna kam hai ki aapko lagega koi scam hai. Par scam nahi hai sir — bas maalik NRI hai, khaali nahi rakhna chahta.

Mujhe lagna chahiye tha ki kuch gadbad hai. Jab koi bole "scam nahi hai" toh usually scam hota hai.

Par Aarav ko kya — usse toh bas ek jagah chahiye thi jaha WiFi aaye aur raat ko sannata ho. Usse mila — B-204, Vasant Residency. Second floor. Balcony ke saamne ek neem ka ped. Aur andar — ek surprisingly clean flat jo kisi ne recently saaf karwaya tha.

Keys mili. Agreement sign hua. Pandey ji ne "best decision sir" bol ke 2 minute me gayab ho gaye.

Aarav ne pehle din ka poora plan banaya tha.

Suitcase kholo. Kapde almari me rakho. Desk set karo — laptop, tablet, drawing pad. Kitchen check karo. Maggi ka stock check karo. Wifi password dhundho. Aur phir — pehli raat ko naye ghar me chai pi ke khidki ke bahar dekho.

Simple plan. Peaceful plan.

Plan tab tak chala jab tak usne bedroom ka darwaza khola — aur andar kisi ka suitcase pada tha.

Pink suitcase. Uske upar ek tote bag. Aur bed pe ek dupatta rakha hua tha — jaise koi abhi abhi gaya ho.

Ye kya hai? Galat flat me aa gaya kya?

Usne key check kiya — B-204. Agreement check kiya — B-204. Pandey ji ko call kiya — switched off.

Tabhi main door khula.

Ek ladki andar aayi. Haath me grocery ka bag, kaan me earphones, aur face pe wo wala expression jo bolta hai "main apni duniya me hu, disturb mat karo."

Usne Aarav ko dekha.
