Silence

Mr. X ko silence kabhi pasand nahi thi.

Wo us type ka insaan tha jo padhayi karte waqt bhi music lagata tha, akele chalte waqt kisi ko call karta tha, 2 AM pe bina reason memes bhejta tha — sirf isliye kyuki silence bohot heavy lagti thi usse. Silence ka matlab tha kuch galat hai. Silence ka matlab tha koi upset hai. Silence ka matlab tha duniya uske bina aage badh rahi hai.

Par aaj raat — andhere kamre me, curtains band, phone ki roshni face pe — silence hi uske paas tha.

Last chat abhi bhi open thi. Uska naam upar. Green dot gayab. Typing indicator jo kabhi wapas nahi aayega.

Ms. Y
Apna khayal rakhna, X.

Bas itna.

Na koi explanation. Na koi fight. Na koi filmy goodbye jisme kuch feel ho. Na "main hamesha tumse pyaar karungi" na "shayad kisi aur janam me." Kuch bhi nahi tha jise wo pakad sake.

Bas paanch shabd. Aur ek ending.

Usne message phir se padha. Aur phir se. Upar scroll kiya — mahino ki baatein, memes, voice notes, raat ke "jaag rahe ho?" wale texts, un paragraphs ke baare me jo kuch nahi the par sab kuch the. Sab kuch abhi bhi wahi tha. Har shabd preserved — jaise kisi museum me rakhi hui cheez jo kabhi zinda thi.

Kuch itna real achanak… kaise ruk jaata hai?

Phone lock kiya. Ceiling dekhi. Phir unlock karke message dobara padha.

Apna khayal rakhna, X.

Jaise ye ab possible tha.

The Beginning

Shuruaat waisi hui jaisi zyada tar cheezein hoti hain — randomly.

Ek mutual friend ka group chat. Kisi ne meme bheja. Ms. Y ne aisa reply kiya — itna unexpectedly funny — ki X class me zor se has pada. Usne screenshot liya aur usse privately bhej diya — "ye actually accha tha lol." Usne reply kiya "lol? bas itna hi aata hai tumhe?"

Aur bas. Yahi shuruaat thi sab kuch ki.

Pehla hafta casual tha. Memes idhar udhar. Random opinions random cheezon pe. Usne bola pizza pe pineapple "elite" hai. Usne bola wo "clinically pagal" hai. Usne 47 second ka voice note bheja explain karte hue ki wo galat hai. Usne teen baar suna — argument ki wajah se nahi, par uski hansi ki wajah se jo end me aayi thi.

Ms. Y
tum literally sabse boring texter ho jo maine kabhi dekha hai
Mr. X
phir bhi roz mujhe text karti ho
Ms. Y
entertainment purposes only
Mr. X
sure

Dusre hafte tak conversations lambi hone lagi. Teesre hafte tak roz baat hone lagi. Pehle mahine ke end tak — dono me se koi bhi bina goodnight bole so nahi paata tha.

X ko exactly pata nahi tha kab shift hua. Koi ek moment nahi tha jaha usne socha "mujhe ye pasand hai." Ye ek dheemi leharon jaisa tha — itni dheere utha ki jab tak notice kiya, tab tak doob chuka tha.

Wo apni terrace se aasmaan ki random photo bhejti aur wo 2 minute tak dekhta rehta. Aasmaan sundar tha isliye nahi — par kyuki usne dekha aur usse share karne ka socha. Ye kuch matlab rakhta tha. Kya exactly — ye nahi pata tha. Par kuch toh tha.

Ms. Y
ye sunset dekho. insane hai.
Mr. X
nice
Ms. Y
"nice"? main tumhe art bhej rahi hu aur tum "nice" bol rahe ho?
Mr. X
kya bolu phir
Ms. Y
kuch emotion ke saath? tum kabhi kabhi deewar lagte ho
Mr. X
deewar jisse tum roz baat karti ho
Ms. Y
…chup raho

Jab bhi uske paas comeback nahi hota — "chup raho" bolti thi. Ye baat usse bohot pyaari lagti thi bina kisi reason ke.

Golden Days

Agle kuch mahine us type ke the jinke baare me log gaane likhte hain.

Grand nahi. Cinematic nahi. Bas… warm. Us tarah ka warm jo tumhe bhula deta hai ki sardi exist karti hai.

Wo ek saath chaos aur calm thi. Apne din ke baare me 15 minute tak non-stop bolti thi — har detail, har annoyance, har chhoti interaction ko dissect karti — aur phir achanak chup ho jaati aur kuch itna soft bol deti ki X ko guard pe pakad leti.

Ms. Y
pata hai mujhe tumme kya accha lagta hai?
Mr. X
kya
Ms. Y
tum actually sunte ho. sachchi me. zyada tar log bas apni baari ka wait karte hain bolne ke liye. tum nahi karte.
Mr. X
shayad mere paas bolne ke liye kuch nahi hota
Ms. Y
dekha? compliments ko goli ki tarah dodge karte ho. accept karo — tum acche insaan ho.

Usne ek minute tak reply nahi kiya. Busy tha isliye nahi. Par kyuki kisi ne ye baat usse pehle kabhi nahi boli thi — aur mean ki thi.

Uske hone se har din halka lagta tha. Monday ko Monday jaisa nahi lagta tha. Bure dino ka ek safety net tha — pata tha ki shaam ko wo call karegi aur kisi tarah duniya balance ho jayegi.

Uski ek aadat thi — type karne me aalas aane pe voice notes bhejti thi. 30 second, kabhi 2 minute. Bas uski awaaz — khaane ke baare me, kisi show ke baare me, kisi sapne ke baare me. Usne har ek save kiya tha. Jaanbujh ke nahi. Bas kabhi delete nahi kiya.

Late nights unki cheez ban gayi thi. Raat 11 ke baad unki duniya thi — koi aur exist nahi karta tha.

Mr. X
3 baj rahe hain. so jao.
Ms. Y
pehle tum
Mr. X
neend nahi aa rahi
Ms. Y
jhooth. tumhare replies slow ho rahe hain. aadhe so rahe ho.
Mr. X
shayad
Ms. Y
jao so jao. main kal bhi hu yahi.
Mr. X
promise?
Ms. Y
promise. goodnight, stupid.

Har raat aise hi khatam hoti thi. "Goodnight, stupid." Teen mahine. Bina ek raat skip kiye.

Aur dheere dheere — bina kisi ne zubaan pe laaye — wo ghar ban gayi.

Jagah nahi. Building nahi. Ek feeling. Wo wali jo seene me rehti hai aur jiske kho jaane ka darr hamesha lagta hai.

The Shift

Pyaar hamesha shor se nahi tuttha.

Na badal garajte hain. Na dramatic scene hota hai. Na darwaze bajte hain. Kabhi kabhi pyaar chup chaap crack hota hai — jaise ek glass jo table ke edge pe pada ho. Girne ki awaaz nahi sunaayi deti. Bas ek din uthte ho aur realize karte ho ki toot chuka hai.

Chhota sa shuru hua. Itna chhota ki X almost miss kar deta.

Reply late aane lage. Bohot late nahi — bas 2 minute ki jagah 20 minute. Phir ek ghanta. Phir "sorry, busy thi." Phir reply ki jagah sirf ek reaction emoji.

Uske texts chhote hone lage. Jaha paragraphs bhejti thi — ab one-liners aane lage. Jaha voice notes aati thi — ab "haha" ya "true" aata tha. Jaha raat 11 baje sharp call aati thi — ab text aata "aaj thaki hui hu, kal baat karte hain?"

Kal aaya. Par call nahi aayi.

X ne sab notice kiya. Wo aisa hi bana tha — usne notice kiya jab usne profile picture badli, jab wo online thi par usse text nahi kar rahi thi, jab uski "haha" me usual exclamation mark nahi tha. Har shift ko catalogue kiya jaise ek scientist toofaan ko aate dekhta hai.

Par bola kuch nahi.

Kyuki darr gaya tha. Sachchi me darr gaya tha. Ki agar pucha "theek ho?" — toh jawab ho sakta hai "shayad hume baat karna band kar dena chahiye."

Toh usne wahi kiya jo dar lagni wali cheezon ke saath hamesha karta tha. Pretend kiya ki sab theek hai. Uski energy match ki — chhota reply, double text nahi kiya, pucha nahi ki calls kyun band hain. Khud ko bola ye normal hai. Log busy hote hain. Life hoti hai.

Mr. X
hey, din kaisa tha?

Seen 4:23 PM. Koi reply nahi.

Mr. X
sab theek?
Ms. Y
haan. bas thak gayi hu. baad me baat karte hain?

"Baad me baat karte hain" uski signature line ban gayi. Baad me kabhi nahi aaya.

Ek raat purane chats upar scroll kiye. Teen mahine pehle ke messages padhe — jokes, 2 AM ki baatein, "goodnight stupid." Aisa laga jaise kisi aur ki kahani padh raha ho. Jaise wo do log — jinki baat band nahi hoti thi — chup chaap kamre se nikal gaye aur unki jagah do ajnabi aa gaye jo ek dusre ke saath polite ho rahe hain.

Ye sabse zalim ending hoti hai. Wo nahi jo phatakti hai. Wo jo udd jaati hai.

Her Side

Ye baat koi nahi batata us insaan ke baare me jo door jaata hai — aksar wahi sabse zyada toot raha hota hai.

Ms. Y theek nahi thi. Hafton se nahi thi.

Ghar se shuru hua. Pressure jiske baare me kabhi baat nahi ki — expectations jo deewar jaisi band ho rahi thi, ghar ki baatein jo usse khaali chhod deti thi, ek version of herself jo har din perform karni padti thi. Sab me se smile karke nikli. Usse ye aata tha. 12 saal ki umr se practice kar rahi thi.

X ko nahi pata tha. Isliye nahi ki trust nahi tha — par kyuki wo bol nahi paa rahi thi. Kaise bolo kisi ko "meri zindagi bikhar rahi hai" jab wo sochta hai ki tum sabse strong insaan ho? Us image ko kaise todo?

Usse uski fikar thi. Zaroorat se zyada. Wahi problem tha.

Kyuki jab tum kisi ki itni fikar karo aur khud ko bikharte feel karo — toh pehla instinct reach out karna nahi hota. Pehla instinct hota hai unhe wreckage se bachana. Wo unki conversations dekhti aur sochti — "ye kisi better insaan ka haqdar hai. Kisi aisa jo poora ho. Kisi aisa jo mushkil se sambhal nahi raha."

Usse lagta tha wo uske laayak nahi hai. Dramatic tarike se nahi. Us chup, ghutan wale tarike se jisme tum believe karne lagte ho ki tum ek bojh ho — jab ho nahi.

Toh usne wo kiya jo usse sahi laga. Peeche hat gayi. Dheere dheere. Aahista se. Jaise bandage nikaalte hain — sochke ki agar dheere karo toh kam dard hoga.

Reply jaanbujh ke late karti thi — baat nahi karni thi isliye nahi, par kyuki wapas type karna jhooth jaisa lagta tha. Kaise bhejo "main theek hu" jab hafton se theek nahi ho? Kaise memes pe haso jab ek ghante pehle ro rahi thi? Sab normal hone ka natak usse suffocate kar raha tha.

Toh usne distance choose kiya. Pyaar karna band hua isliye nahi.

Par kyuki usse baad me aur takleef nahi dena chahti thi.

Kuch raatein aisi thi jab wo uske liye lambe messages type karti. Sab kuch — ghar wali baatein, pressure, doobne wali feeling, door hone ki wajah. 10 minute tak type karti, aankhein dhundli, seena bhaari.

Phir wapas padhti. Aur sab delete kar deti.

Agar usne fix karne ki koshish ki toh? Agar wo majboori se ruka toh? Agar mera chaos uska chaos ban gaya toh? Wo ye deserve nahi karta.

Usne khud ko bola ki jaana pyaar ki ek shakal hai. Ki door dhakalna usse bachana hai.

Shayad tha. Shayad nahi tha. Par 2 AM pe jab doob rahi ho — tab clearly nahi sochte. Bas koshish karte ho ki jinse pyaar karte ho unhe paani ke upar rakho — chahe iske liye unka haath chhodni pade.

The Last Night

Teen hafte "baad me baat karte hain" aur aadhe replies ke. Teen hafte X ka pretend karna ki notice nahi kiya. Teen hafte Y ka pretend karna ki toot nahi rahi.

Phir ek Tuesday raat 11:47 pe — X finally toota.

Mr. X
ek baat puchu?
Ms. Y
haan
Mr. X
maine kuch galat kiya kya?

Message 11:48 pe deliver hua. Usne 11:48 pe padha. Typing indicator 11:49 pe aaya.

Phir gayab ho gaya.

11:51 pe phir aaya. Gayab.

11:53. 11:55. 11:57. Indicator naachta raha — aata, jaata — jaise uske shabd exist hona chahte the par ho nahi paa rahe the.

Uski taraf — screen blur thi. Hafton se roki hui aansuon finally phone ki screen pe gir rahi thi. Chup chaap.

Usne type kiya: "I'm sorry. Kuch chal raha hai mere saath aur mujhe nahi pata kaise baat karu. Tumhari galti nahi hai. Kuch bhi tumhari galti nahi hai. Tum sabse acchi cheez ho jo mere saath hui aur isliye main—"

Delete.

Phir type kiya: "Tumne kuch galat nahi kiya. Maine kiya. Maine tumhe door dhakela kyuki mujhe darr hai ki tumhe hurt karungi. Kyuki meri zindagi mess hai aur nahi chahti ki tumhari bhi ban jaaye. Kyuki tum kisi better ke—"

Delete.

Phir type kiya: "I love you. Aur isliye mujhe jaana hoga."

Delete.

12:04 AM. Chhe minute ke drafts jo kabhi pahunche nahi.

Finally usne bheja:

Ms. Y
Nahi… tumne sab kuch sahi kiya.

X ne screen dekhta raha. Us sentence me kuch tha — uska weight, uski finality — jisne uska pet gir gaya.

Mr. X
phir kya ho raha hai? baat karo mujhse. please.

Ek lambi chuppi. Uski zindagi ki sabse lambi chuppi.

Phir:

Ms. Y
Apna khayal rakhna, X.

Usne turant type kiya — "iska matlab kya hai?" "Y please." "ye mat karo." "kam se kam bata do kyu."

Sab deliver hue. Koi read nahi hua.

Usne phone band kar diya tha. Ulta karke desk pe rakh diya. Kambal sar pe kheench liya. Aur tab tak royi jab tak kuch bacha nahi.

Do log. Ek screen se alag. Dono ro rahe the. Dono reach out karna chahte the. Dono convinced the ki chuppi zyada kind option hai.

Aftermath

X ne wo last message us raat 50 baar padha. Phir agle din subah 50 baar aur. Phir uske agle din 50 baar aur.

Har shabd todaa. "Apna khayal rakhna" — "apna" kyu? Matlab wo nahi rakhegi? "X" — na "stupid," na uska nickname. Bas naam. Thanda. Final. Jaise kisi document pe signature.

Pehla hafta sabse bura tha. Har 10 minute pe phone uthata. Dekhta ki wo online hai kya. Dekhta ki messages dekhe kya. Profile picture check karta — wahi hai? Badli kya? Badli toh matlab kya hai? Nahi badli toh matlab kya hai?

Messages type kiye jo kabhi bheje nahi:

"Mujhe nahi jaanna kya hua. Bas bata do ki theek ho."

"Main ruk jaata. Kuch bhi ho. Ye toh pata hai na tumhe?"

"Tumne bola tha 'main kal bhi hu yahi.' Kal ko kya hua?"

Sab type kiye. Sab delete kiye. Ye usne ussi se seekha tha apparently.

Doston ne notice kiya. "Tu chup hai." "Kha nahi raha." "Y ke saath kya hua?"

Usne bola "kuch nahi." Jo technically sach tha. Kuch hua nahi. Wahi toh problem thi. Na fight. Na reason. Na closure. Bas ek darwaza jo itna dheere band hua ki awaaz bhi nahi aayi.

Kisi ne bola — "bhai, move on kar. Aur bhi log hain duniya me."

Usne smile kiya. Nod kiya. Aur socha: Tum nahi samjhoge. Wo "log" nahi thi. Wo wo thi. Aur uske jaisi sirf ek hai.

Dusre hafte usne check karna band kar diya ki wo online hai ya nahi. Fikar karna band hua isliye nahi — par kyuki "last seen 2 ghante pehle" dekhna aur ye jaanna ki wo zinda hai par usse reach out nahi kar rahi — ye na jaanne se bhi bura tha.

Teesre hafte purane messages scroll karna band kar diya. Yaadein mitne isliye nahi — par kyuki dard deti thi. Har "goodnight stupid" ab ek chaku tha. Har voice note jo play karne ki himmat nahi thi.

Chauthe hafte… bas exist karta tha. Class gaya. Ghar aaya. Khaaya. Soya. Repeat. Jaise ek machine default settings pe chal rahi ho bina kisi drive ke.

Log bolte hain time heal karta hai. Par wo nahi batate ki healing wale din kaise feel hote hain — khaali, lamba khinche hue, bina rang ke. Time heal nahi karta. Bas sikha deta hai weight kaise carry karna hai.

Aur wo?

Wo bhi wahi kar rahi thi. Exist kar rahi thi. Motions se guzar rahi thi. Khud ko bol rahi thi ki sahi kiya. Kuch din believe karti thi. Zyada tar din nahi karti thi.

Uski friend Mira ne ek din pucha — "miss karti hai usse?"

Usne bola "nahi."

Phir ghar aayi aur uski chat ko ek ghante tak dekhti rahi bina open kiye. Kyuki open karne se "read" dikh jaata. Aur wo itni umeed nahi de sakti thi. Jab wapas aa sakegi ye sure nahi tha.

Kisi se pyaar karna aur unse door chale jaana — ye sabse mushkil kaam hai jo koi kar sakta hai. Jaana mushkil isliye nahi. Par kyuki har din phir se jaane ka choice karna padta hai. Baar baar.

What Ifs

Mahine guzre.

Teez dard dhundla ho gaya. Roz ki takleef hafta wari ban gayi. Phir random — achanak aa jaati unexpected moments pe. Koi gaana jo usne sunaya tha. Coffee ki smell us exact waqt jab call karti thi. Ek meme jo automatically sochta "isse ye funny lagta" phir yaad aata ki bhejne ke liye koi nahi hai.

X abhi bhi kabhi kabhi uski chat open karta hai.

Text karne ke liye nahi. Wo us se aage hai. Chat open karta hai, uska naam dekhta hai, last message dekhta hai — "Apna khayal rakhna, X" — aur bas ek moment ke liye uske saath baithta hai. Jaise kisi qabr pe jaana. Kisi cheez ko respect dena jo kabhi zinda thi.

Kabhi kabhi sochta hai — agar pehle pucha hota toh? Agar pehli baar jab late reply aayi, tab bola hota "pata hai kuch off hai. Batana zaruri nahi. Par main hu yaha." Kya cheezein alag hoti?

Ya agar wo aakhri message bhej deta? Wo wala jo bola "mujhe farak nahi padta kya chal raha hai tumhare saath. Main nahi ja raha. Ye decide karne ka haq tumhe nahi hai."

Kya wo ruk jaati? Ya aur door dhakelti?

Kabhi nahi pata chalega. "What if" ka yahi sabse zalim hissa hai — kabhi jawab nahi deta. Sirf aur sawaal deta hai.

Aur Y?

Wo abhi bhi kabhi kabhi search bar me uska naam type karti hai. Uski chat pe thumb hover karta hai. Last few messages padhti hai — uske "please" aur "kam se kam bata do kyu" jo kabhi open nahi kiye.

Kabhi kabhi almost call kar deti hai. Raat 11 baje. Purani aadat. Ungli button pe tikti hai 3 second, 5 second, 10 second.

Phir phone lock kar deti hai.

Khud ko bolti hai aise hi better hai. Mostly convinced hai. Mostly.

Dono ek hi sheher me rehte hain. Ek hi hawa me saans lete hain. Wahi sunsets dekhte hain jo wo photograph karti thi. Aise raston pe chalte hain jo shayad 15 minute ki duri pe hain. Par wo 15 minute samundar bhi ho sakte hain.

Shayad ek din takra jayenge. Kisi chai ki dukaan pe. Kisi bookstore me. Kisi random gali ke kone pe jaha universe dramatic hone ka decide kare.

Shayad wo bolegi "hi." Shayad wo bolega "hey." Shayad ek moment ke liye wahi khade rahenge — do log jinhe ek waqt pe ek dusre ke baare me sab pata tha, ab kuch safe bolne ke liye dhundh rahe hain.

Ya shayad milenge hi nahi. Shayad bas yahi hai. Shayad kuch kahaniyaan beech me khatam ho jaati hain, bina aakhri chapter ke, bina saaf ending ke. Bas do log ek hi zakhm ko alag alag directions me le jaate hain.

The Last Message

Kuch love stories hate se nahi khatam hoti. Na fights se, na darwaaze bajne se, na pulon ko jalane se.


Wo silence se khatam hoti hain.

"What ifs" se.

Do logon se jinhe abhi bhi farak padta hai…

…par ruk nahi paaye.
