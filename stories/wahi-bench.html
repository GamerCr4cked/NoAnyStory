Pehli Nazar

8th class ka ek normal sa din tha. Wahi classroom, wahi smell — chalk dust aur purane desks ki lakdi. Ceiling fan aadha ghoom raha tha, jaise usse bhi bore ho raha ho. Teacher board pe kuch likh rahe the jo shayad important tha — par Jai ke liye bilkul nahi tha.

Wo apni usual jagah pe tha — last bench ke aas paas, pen ghumata hua, kabhi khidki ke bahar dekhta, kabhi rough notebook me kuch random draw karta. Aaj ka drawing — ek spaceship jo kisi aur duniya me ja rahi thi. Kyunki ye duniya toh boring thi.

Zindagi me kuch exciting nahi chal raha tha. Har din same. Uthna, school, ghar, sona. Repeat. Jaise koi ne copy-paste kar diya har din ko.

Uske dost Sahil uske bagal me baitha tha — half sleeping, half pretending to write.

Jai
bhai ye period kab khatam hoga
pen ghumata raha, nazar ghadi pe gayi — abhi 20 minute baki the
Sahil

Jai ne halki si hasi di — dheemi, taaki teacher na sune. Phir wapas khidki ke bahar dekhne laga.

Bas aise hi — bina kisi reason ke — bina kisi intention ke — uski nazar girls row ki taraf gayi.

Aur waha usne usko dekha.

Aarohi.

Wo apni seat pe baithi thi — second row, khidki wali side. Sar jhuka ke likh rahi thi. Baal ek taraf gire hue the. Pen steady chal raha tha — jaise usse exactly pata tha kya likhna hai.

Pehle kuch khaas feel nahi hua. Simple si ladki thi. Quiet. Apne kaam me busy. Na koi fancy style, na koi attention-grabbing attitude. Bas ek aur face tha classroom me.

Jai ne us moment ko seriously liya bhi nahi. Nazar gayi, aur hata li. Normal.

Main socha — bas aise hi dekh liya. Koi baat nahi. Kal yaad bhi nahi rahega.

Par galat tha.

Din beetne lage. Hafta beet gaya. Phir ek aur hafta.

Aur dheere dheere… bina pata chale… wo ek "normal face" se kuch aur banne lagi.

Aarohi topper thi. Har kaam perfect. Har notebook complete — wahi notebook jisme wo ek alag duniya likhti thi. Aur jab bhi koi help maangta — wo mana nahi karti thi. Kabhi gussa nahi, kabhi "abhi nahi" nahi. Bas de deti thi — apna time, apni notes, apni help. Bina kuch expect kiye.

Ek din English ka period tha. Teacher ne ek paragraph padhne ko bola — loudly, class ke saamne. Kisi ki himmat nahi thi. Sab chup.

Aarohi ka haath utha.

Wo khadi hui — copy haath me, thodi nervous par steady — aur padhne lagi.

Uski awaaz bahut halki thi. Jaise wo hamesha dheere bolti ho — par itni clearly ki har shabd samajh aata tha. Class me sannata tha. Sab sun rahe the — par Jai kuch alag sun raha tha.

Pata nahi kya tha us awaaz me. Par mujhe laga… main ye aur sunna chahta hu.

Jab wo baithne lagi, ek second ke liye uski nazar Jai ki taraf aayi — probably by accident. Jai ne jaldi se neeche dekh liya, jaise kuch kar raha ho.

Kuch nahi hua tha. Par kuch feel hua tha.

Kabhi kabhi wo apni copy me kuch alag likhti rehti — teacher ki baat nahi, kuch apna. Shayad stories likhti thi, shayad diary, shayad koi poem. Aur us waqt uska expression… alag hi hota tha. Jaise wo duniya se disconnect ho jaati thi aur apni kisi aur jagah chali jaati thi. Lips thode hil rahe hote — jaise mann me padh rahi ho. Kabhi halka sa smile aa jaata — kisi line pe jo acchi lagi hogi.

Aur jab wo likhti thi… Jai dekhta rehta tha. Chup chap. Door se. Jaise koi painting dekh raha ho — chhuna nahi hai, bas dekhna hai.

Ek din Sahil ne Jai ko pakad liya.

Kya itna obvious hu? Nahi nahi… Sahil pagal hai. Main kisi ko nahi dekh raha.

Jhooth bol raha tha. Khud se.

Jai apni duniya me mast tha. Doston ke saath mazaak, class me bakchodi, cricket ke arguments, aur phir bhi somehow numbers aa jaate the. Sab normal tha.

Bas ek cheez alag thi.

Kabhi beech me, bina reason, bina warning — uski nazar usi taraf chali jaati.

Kabhi wo likh rahi hoti. Kabhi kisi ko samjha rahi hoti. Kabhi bas khidki ke bahar dekh rahi hoti — chin haath pe rakhe, kuch sochte hue.

Aur har baar — har baar — Jai ko lagta jaise time thoda slow ho gaya ho. Jaise classroom ka shor thoda dheema ho gaya ho. Jaise fan ka ghoomna ruk gaya ho.

Pehle wo "thik" thi. Phir "acchi" lagne lagi. Aur phir… pata hi nahi chala kab wo important ban gayi.

Usne kabhi kisi ko bataya nahi. Na Sahil ko. Na kisi aur dost ko. Na khud ko properly accept kiya.

Ye kya ho raha hai mujhe? Main toh normal tha. Ye sab toh filmon me hota hai. Mujhe nahi hona chahiye.

Par feelings waha thi. Saaf, seedhi, aur chup. Jaise koi dheemi si dhun baj rahi ho — sirf usse sunai de rahi thi.

Chhoti Cheezein

Pura saal chal raha tha aur un dono ke beech koi badi baat nahi hui thi. Na koi deep conversation, na koi secret message, na koi dramatic moment. Na koi filmy scene jisme barish me dono ek chhate ke neeche aaye. Na koi accidental touch. Kuch nahi.

Bas chhoti chhoti cheezein thi — itni chhoti ki koi aur notice bhi nahi karta. Duniya ke liye wo invisible moments the.

Par Jai ke liye… wo sab kuch thi.

Ek din Jai ki History ki copy nahi mili. Teacher ne bola "kisi se le lo." Sab apni copies chhupa rahe the — kyunki kisi ko share nahi karni thi. Jai ne idhar udhar dekha — aur pata nahi kyu, bina soche, paer khud ba khud Aarohi ki taraf chale gaye.

Phir ek din lunch break me Jai corridor me chal raha tha. Sahil ke saath cricket ka argument chal raha tha — Kohli ya Rohit, usual bakwaas.

Aarohi saamne se aa rahi thi — apni friend ke saath baat karte hue. Kuch has rahi thi — halka sa, dheema sa — jaise koi inside joke ho.

Jab paas se guzri, ek second ke liye — bilkul ek second ke liye — uski nazar Jai pe aayi.

Aur wo halki si muskuraayi.

Itni chhoti smile thi. Itni chhoti ki shayad smile thi bhi nahi — shayad bas uski pehle ki hansi ka tail end tha. Par Jai ne us chhoti si cheez ko pakad liya jaise koi drowning insaan lifeboat pakadta hai.

Agar koi aur hota toh notice bhi nahi karta. Par Jai ne notice kiya. Kyunki wo hamesha notice karta tha.

Sahil bol raha tha — "bhai sun, Kohli ka average dekh" — par Jai ko kuch sunai nahi de raha tha. Uske kaan me bas ek hi sound thi — apni dhadkan.

Wo smile uske dimaag me ghanto tak rahi. Class me bhi. Ghar jaake bhi. Raat ko bed pe lete hue bhi — wahi ek second replay ho raha tha, baar baar, slow motion me.

Kya wo muskuraayi mere liye thi? Ya bas normal thi? Ya shayad wo kisi aur baat pe has rahi thi? Par usne MERI taraf dekha tha na? Ya nahi dekha? Main imagine toh nahi kar raha?

Overthink. Hamesha overthink. Par band nahi ho raha tha.

Ek aur din. Maths ka period. Teacher ne group kaam diya — benches ke hisaab se pairs ban gaye.

Jai ki kismat — ya uski naakismat — Aarohi ke group me daala gaya.

Jab teacher ne naam announce kiye, Jai ka dil ek beat chhod gaya. Sahil ne peeche se uski peeth pe thapki maari aur chup chap haste hue thumbs up dikhaaya. Jai ne death stare diya.

Pehli baar itne paas baitha tha. Desk milaayi gayi — uski copy uske saamne, Jai ki copy uske saamne. Beech me barely ek haath ka gap. Uska perfume — nahi, shayad shampoo — halka sa tha. Coconut jaisa kuch. Par Jai ko lag raha tha poori class me bas wahi smell hai.

"Fast ho tum." Usse lagta hai main smart hu. Nahi nahi, bas aise hi bol diya. Par tone me kuch tha na? Nahi, normal tha. Par smile toh ki thi na…

2 ghante. 2 ghante teen shabdon pe. Ye haal tha.

Ek din barish ho rahi thi. School khatam hua par barish rukne ka naam nahi le rahi thi. Mausam grey tha — wo wali grey jo thodi sad lagti hai par thodi acchi bhi. Patte bheeg ke chikne ho gaye the, aur school ki chhath se paani tapak raha tha jaise koi dhara bana di ho.

Bahut bacche gate pe khade the — chhata nahi tha kisi ke paas. Kuch bacche bag sar pe rakh ke bhaag gaye. Kuch phone pe parents ko call kar rahe the.

Jai bhi wahi khada tha. Aur — wahi — Aarohi bhi.

Dono ke beech 4-5 log khade the. Par Jai ko sirf wo dikh rahi thi. Wo apna bag seene se lagaake khadi thi — phone nahi tha haath me, bas barish dekh rahi thi. Baal thode geele ho gaye the kinaron se. Wo unhe kaan ke peeche kar rahi thi — baar baar — par wo wapas aa jaate the.

Jai wo chhoti si harkat dekh raha tha — baal kaan ke peeche, wapas, kaan ke peeche, wapas — jaise koi loop ho. Aur usse lag raha tha ye duniya ki sabse acchi cheez hai.

Jai chahta tha kuch bole. Kuch bhi. "Barish tez hai na?" — itna simple sa sentence. 6 shabd. Bas 6 shabd bolne the.

Kya bolu? Kaise bolu? Wo sochegi main weird hu. Itni barish me random baat kyu kar raha hai. Rehne de. Chup reh. Tu aise hi theek hai.

10 minute baad barish thodi kam hui. Aarohi ne bag adjust kiya aur chali gayi. Uski friend kahi se aayi aur dono bhaagte hue gate ke bahar nikle.

Jai wahi khada raha. Barish phir tez ho gayi. Wo bheeg gaya. Par barish ki wajah se nahi.

Ek din PT period tha. Ground pe sab the. Boys cricket khel rahe the, girls side me baithi thi kuch gossip karte hue.

Jai batting kar raha tha — ya karne ki koshish kar raha tha. Par aaj uska dhyaan bilkul nahi tha. Kyunki boundary ke paas — ground ke kone me — Aarohi apni friend ke saath baithi thi. Kuch baat kar rahi thi, kabhi has rahi thi, kabhi grass ke tinkke tod rahi thi.

Homework ka ek aur din. Jai ne peeche se Aarohi ko "Homework kya hai?" pucha — ye uska naya bahana tha baat karne ka.

Bas. Ye thi unki baat. "Copy milegi?" "Homework kya hai?" "Thanks." Ye tha unka poora relationship — agar isse relationship bol sakte hain.

Chhoti cheezein. Har ek forgettable — duniya ke liye. Par Jai ke liye — ek ek moment ek ek memory tha jo wo apne andar aise sambhaal ke rakhta tha jaise glass ka koi naazon se tootne wala cheez ho.

Kyunki jab koi tumhe accha lagta hai, toh uski har "haan le lo" ek gift lagti hai. Uski har smile ek chapter ban jaati hai. Aur uski har chhoti si baat — tumhari poori raat ki neend le jaati hai.

Wo Letter

Saal khatam hone wala tha. March aa gaya tha. Exams paas the. Sab padhai ki tension me the.

Par Jai ki tension alag thi.

Uske andar ek jung chal rahi thi — bol du ya nahi? Keh du ya chup rahu? Kya fayda bolne ka? Aur kya fayda chup rehne ka?

Ye sawaal mahino se chal raha tha. Har raat. Har baar jab wo usse dekhta. Har baar jab nazar milti — ya nahi milti.

Agar nahi bola toh yahi regret rahega — "kaash bol deta." Zindagi bhar ye sochta rahunga. Aur agar bola toh shayad reject ho jaunga. Par kam se kam try toh karunga. Kam se kam ye guilt nahi rahega.

Saalon baad.

Jai ab bada ho gaya hai. School khatam. College shuru. Naye log, nayi jagah, naya sheher. Sab badal gaya — chehra thoda, awaaz thodi, sochne ka tarika bohot.

Ek din — shayad coincidence tha, shayad nahi — Jai wapas apne purane school ke paas se guzra. Ghar aa raha tha kisi kaam se. Auto se utra, aur achanak dekha — wahi gate. Wahi deewarein. Wahi peepal ka ped jo gate ke bahar tha.

Canteen ke paas se guzra. Wahi jagah jaha wo door se dekhta tha — apne haathon me tiffin, par bhook nahi.

Aur phir — stairs chadha. First floor. Left turn.

Classroom.

THE END

Ye kahani un logon ke liye hai jinhe kabhi kisi ne accha laga — aur wo bol nahi paaye. Ya bole aur jawab "nahi" aaya. Ye kahani un logon ke liye hai jinki pehli feeling — pehla crush, pehla pyaar, pehla dard — kisi ko yaad bhi nahi, par unhe aaj bhi yaad hai.

Jai ne kuch galat nahi kiya. Aarohi ne bhi kuch galat nahi kiya. Koi villain nahi tha is kahani me. Bas — timing galat thi. Umr chhoti thi. Aur duniya bohot badi.

Par iska ye matlab nahi ki wo feelings fake thi. Chhoti umr ka pyaar "sirf crush" nahi hota — wo utna hi real hota hai. Utna hi dard deta hai. Utna hi yaad rehta hai.

Agar tumne kabhi kisi bench pe baithe baithe kisi ko dekha hai — door se, chupke se, bina bolke — toh ye kahani tumhari hai.

Aur agar tum aaj bhi kabhi kabhi us bench ke baare me sochte ho — toh jaano ki tum akele nahi ho.

Bohot log hain. Bohot benches hain. Bohot kahaniyaan hain jo kabhi complete nahi hui.

Par feel hui. Bohot feel hui.

Aur shayad — yahi kaafi hai.
