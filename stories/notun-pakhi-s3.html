12th Wala

12th class. Last year of school life — technically college section, par campus wahi. Ek saal pehle tak main wo ladka tha jo door se dekhta tha. Ab main wo ladka hu jo roz usse milta hai, usse hasa ta hai, aur raat ko 10:59 tak uska message wait karta hai.

Akhi ab 9th me thi. School section me. Ek saal aur badi, ek saal aur confident. Pehle jab wo baat karti thi toh thoda ruk ke sochti thi — ab seedha bolti thi. Bina filter ke. Aur mujhe wo aur bhi accha lagta tha.

12th ka pressure alag tha. Boards, entrance exams, tuitions, aur ghar pe "beta future ka socho" wala daily lecture. Main sochta tha — par future me ek cheez fix thi. Chahe kuch bhi ho, Akhi mere din ka hissa rahegi.

Par ab ek nayi tension thi — agar boards ke baad main kahin aur chala gaya toh? Dusra sheher? Dusra college? Ye sawaal kabhi kabhi raat ko 2 baje dimaag me aata tha aur neend ud jaati thi.

Pehla din — 12th ka. Main corridor me ja raha tha, bag ek shoulder pe, earphones in. Neeche school building se familiar shor aa raha tha — bacchon ki awaazein, bell, teachers ki chillaahat.

Phone pe notification aaya:

Akhi
observation. do saal se kar rahi hu.

Do saal. Usne count rakha tha.

Lunch pe canteen me mile — usual jagah, usual time. Par aaj kuch alag tha. Akhi ne naya hairband lagaya tha — navy blue, simple. Aur baal thode alag tie kiye the.

Maine notice kiya. Par bola nahi.

Pehli baar usne directly accept kiya ki meri baat usse acchi lagti hai. Bina joke me udaye, bina topic change kiye.

Phir maine kuch kiya jo kabhi nahi kiya tha — bina soche, bina plan kiye. Maine apna haath uske haath ke paas rakha. Floor pe. Chhoti si distance me.

Usne neeche dekha. Phir dheere se apni ungli mere haath pe rakhi. Bas ek ungli. Halki si.

Koi baat nahi hui. Koi shabd nahi the. Bas wo ek touch tha — rooftop pe, sunset me, hawa ke beech.

Aur us moment me — poori duniya me sirf hum dono the.

Unsaid Things

Rooftop ke baad sab kuch thoda intense ho gaya — par acche wale intense.

Ab jab milte the toh nazar thodi aur zyada tikti thi. Smile thodi aur gehra hoti thi. Aur wo beech ka gap — physically aur emotionally — dono kam hote ja rahe the.

Ek raat 10:40 pe phone pe baat ho rahi thi. Random topic se shuru hua — school ka ek teacher jo funny hai, canteen ka naya menu, Riya ki bakchodi. Par phir baat ghoom ke wahin aayi jaha hamesha aati thi:

Us din rooftop se neeche aate waqt stairs pe — thoda andhera tha, ek light fused thi — Akhi ka pair phisla.

Main peeche tha. Reflexively uska haath pakda — tezi se, tight grip.

Wo sambhal gayi. Par maine haath nahi chhoda. Aur usne kheencha bhi nahi.

Hum dono stairs pe khade the. Ek tuhi fused light. Bahar se corridor ka halka sa shor. Aur beech me — do haath, ek dusre me.

Akhi

Phir dheere se haath alag hue. Dono neeche aaye. Koi baat nahi ki. Par dono ki ungliyon me ek halki si jhunjhunahat thi — jo ghar tak rahi.

Confession

Uske baad? Pata nahi.

"Tum meri pehli mohabbat ho — nayi ya purani, tum meri ho."

Humara

January. Naye saal ke baad sab kuch officially "humara" ho gaya — bina kisi announcement ke, bina kisi status ke. Na Instagram pe kuch daala, na doston ko formally bataya. Par har koi samajh gaya tha.

Rahul

Rahul

Riya ka reaction bhi aaya — usne Akhi ko message kiya:

Akhi

Ab cheezein pehle se aur different thi.

Pehle canteen me milte the aur thoda dheere baat karte the — ab wo aake mere saath baithti thi naturally, jaise hamesha se aisa hi tha. Pehle phone pe 10:59 tak baat hoti thi — ab kabhi kabhi 11:30 bhi ho jaata (mummy risk pe).

Aur wo chhoti cheezein — wo ab zyada frequently hone lagi thi:

  • Corridor me guzarte waqt uska haath halka sa mere haath ko chhoo jaana
  • Rooftop pe baithe waqt uska sar mere shoulder pe rakh dena
  • Main kuch likhu toh wo paas aake silently padhna — bina kuch bole
  • Aur kabhi kabhi — bilkul achanak — wo meri taraf dekh ke muskurana. Bina reason. Bas aise hi.

Ek January ki dopahar. Rooftop. Main kuch likh raha tha us notebook me jo usne di thi. Wo mere bagal me baithi thi, apna sketchbook liye.

Achanak usne bola:

Akhi

Main freeze ho gaya — literally. 10 minute tak hilna mana tha. Wo concentrate karke draw karti rahi. Kabhi meri taraf dekhti, kabhi sketchbook pe. Uski aankhein mere face ke har detail pe tikti thi — aankhen, naak, hoth, baal.

Kisi ne mujhe kabhi itne dhyan se nahi dekha tha. Aur wo feeling — jab koi tumhe us tarah dekhe jaise tumhe sach me dekhna chahta ho — wo duniya ki sabse addictive feeling hai.

Jab khatam hua, usne dikhaya.

Mere face ka sketch tha — simple lines me, par somehow bilkul main tha. Us sketch me wo sab tha jo mirror me kabhi nahi dikhta — softness, warmth, aur kuch jo shayad sirf usse dikhta tha.

Kabhi kabhi koi tumhe wo dikha deta hai jo tum khud nahi dekh sakte. Aur wahi pyaar hai.

February. Valentine's week. Poora school aur college red aur pink me dooba hua tha. Cringe proposals, chocolate exchanges, aur har taraf "be my valentine" ke posters.

Maine socha kuch karna chahiye — par kya? Main grand gesture wala insaan nahi hu. Filmy nahi hu. Toh maine wo kiya jo main best karta hu.

Likha.

Us notebook ke pehle blank page pe — jo usne di thi — maine ek chhoti si kavita likhi. Raw, unfiltered:

"Tum meri pehli subah ki pehli soch ho,
aur raat ki aakhri smile.
Tum meri shortcut ho — lambi, tedhi,
par har baar tumhi tak jaati hai.
Tum meri Nayi Chidiya ho —
jo kabhi door thi,
par ab itni paas hai
ki dhadkan sun sakti hai."

Valentine's Day pe wo notebook usse wapas di. Bola — "pehla page tumhara tha. Doosra mera."

Usne wahi padha. Railing pe khadi thi. Hawa chal rahi thi.

Padhte waqt uski aankhen bhari ho gayi. Ek aansoo gira — par wo ro nahi rahi thi. Wo smile kar rahi thi. Ek saath.

Akhi

Suresh
kisi ke liye likhne laayak nahi laga tha. ab laga.

Usne notebook apne seene se lagaya. Phir meri taraf dekha — aur kuch nahi boli. Par uski aankhein sab keh rahi thi.

Phir wo mere paas aayi. Bohot paas. Aur usne apna maatha mere shoulder pe rakh diya. Halka sa. Jaise duniya bhar ka weight utaar rahi ho.

Main bhi kuch nahi bola. Bas khada raha. Usse feel karta raha.

Valentine's Day pe na rose tha, na teddy, na ring. Bas ek kavita, ek rooftop, aur do log jo ek dusre ke kareeb khade the — bina kisi reason ke, bina kisi excuse ke.

Growing Up

Boards khatam hue. Results ka wait tha. Par ab ek aur bada sawaal saamne tha — college. Real wala. Sheher se bahar jaana padega kya? Ya yahi milega?
