Pehli Nazar

8th class ka ek normal sa din tha. Wahi classroom, wahi smell — chalk dust aur purane desks ki lakdi. Teacher board pe kuch likh rahe the jo shayad important tha, par Jai ke liye bilkul nahi tha.

Wo apni usual jagah pe tha — last bench ke aas paas, pen ghumata hua, kabhi khidki ke bahar dekhta, kabhi rough notebook me kuch random draw karta.

Zindagi me kuch exciting nahi chal raha tha. Har din same. Uthna, school, ghar, sona. Repeat.

Bas aise hi — bina kisi reason ke — uski nazar girls row ki taraf gayi.

Aur waha usne usko dekha.

Aarohi.

Pehle kuch khaas feel nahi hua. Simple si ladki thi. Quiet. Apne kaam me busy. Na koi fancy style, na koi attention-grabbing attitude. Bas ek aur face tha classroom me.

Jai ne us moment ko seriously liya bhi nahi. Nazar gayi, aur hata li. Normal.

Main socha — bas aise hi dekh liya. Koi baat nahi.

Par din beetne lage.

Aur dheere dheere… bina pata chale… wo ek "normal face" se kuch aur banne lagi.

Aarohi topper thi. Har kaam perfect. Har notebook complete. Aur jab bhi koi help maangta — wo mana nahi karti thi. Kabhi gussa nahi, kabhi "abhi nahi" nahi. Bas de deti thi — apna time, apni notes, apni help.

Uski awaaz bahut halki thi. Jaise wo hamesha dheere bolti ho — par itni clearly ki har shabd samajh aata tha. Aur pata nahi kyu… uski har baat sunne ka mann karta tha.

Kabhi kabhi wo apni copy me kuch alag likhti rehti — teacher ki baat nahi, kuch apna. Shayad stories likhti thi, shayad diary. Aur us waqt uska expression… alag hi hota tha. Jaise wo duniya se disconnect ho jaati thi aur apni kisi aur jagah chali jaati thi.

Aur jab wo likhti thi… Jai dekhta rehta tha. Chup chap. Door se.

Jai apni duniya me mast tha. Doston ke saath mazaak, class me bakchodi, aur phir bhi somehow number aa jaate the. Sab normal tha.

Bas ek cheez alag thi.

Kabhi beech me, bina reason, uski nazar usi taraf chali jaati.

Kabhi wo likh rahi hoti. Kabhi kisi ko samjha rahi hoti. Kabhi bas khidki ke bahar dekh rahi hoti.

Aur har baar — Jai ko lagta jaise time thoda slow ho gaya ho.

Pehle wo "thik" thi. Phir "acchi" lagne lagi. Aur phir… pata hi nahi chala kab wo important ban gayi.

Usne kabhi kisi ko bataya nahi. Na doston ko. Na khud ko properly accept kiya.

Par feelings waha thi. Saaf, seedhi, aur chup.

Chhoti Cheezein

Pura saal chal raha tha aur un dono ke beech koi badi baat nahi hui thi. Na koi deep conversation, na koi secret message, na koi dramatic moment.

Bas chhoti chhoti cheezein thi — itni chhoti ki koi aur notice bhi nahi karta.

Par Jai ke liye… wo sab kuch thi.

Ek din Jai ki History ki copy nahi mili. Teacher ne bola "kisi se le lo." Jai ne idhar udhar dekha — aur bina soche Aarohi ki taraf chala gaya.

Wo apni notebook me kuch likh rahi thi. Bina upar dekhe —

Aarohi

Itna. Bas itna.

Na usne upar dekha. Na smile kiya. Na kuch aur bola. Sirf do shabd — "haan le lo."

Par un do shabdon ne Jai ka poora din bana diya. Wo copy haath me liye wapas gaya aur 5 minute tak usi line pe atka raha — padha kuch nahi, bas uski handwriting dekh raha tha.

Phir ek din lunch break me Jai corridor me chal raha tha. Aarohi saamne se aa rahi thi — apni friend ke saath baat karte hue.

Jab paas se guzri, ek second ke liye — bilkul ek second ke liye — uski nazar Jai pe aai.

Aur wo halki si muskuraayi.

Itni chhoti smile thi ki agar koi aur hota toh notice bhi nahi karta. Par Jai ne notice kiya. Kyuki wo hamesha notice karta tha.

Wo smile uske dimaag me ghanto tak rahi. Raat ko bed pe lete hue bhi wahi soch raha tha — kya wo muskuraayi mere liye thi? Ya bas normal thi? Ya shayad wo kisi aur baat pe has rahi thi?

Overthink. Hamesha overthink. Par band nahi ho raha tha.

Ek aur din. Maths ka period. Teacher ne group kaam diya — benches ke hisaab se pairs ban gaye. Jai ki kismat — ya uski naakismat — Aarohi ke group me daala gaya.

Pehli baar itne paas baitha tha. Uska perfume — nahi, shayad shampoo — halka sa tha, par Jai ko lag raha tha poori class me bas wahi smell hai.

"Fast ho tum."

Teen shabd. Teen shabd jo Jai ne hafton tak yaad rakhe.

Ek din barish ho rahi thi. School khatam hua par barish rukne ka naam nahi le rahi thi. Bahut bacche gate pe khade the — chhata nahi tha kisi ke paas.

Jai bhi wahi khada tha. Aur — wahi — Aarohi bhi.

Dono ke beech 4-5 log khade the. Par Jai ko sirf wo dikh rahi thi. Wo apna bag upar rakh ke khadi thi, phone nahi tha haath me (shayad bag me tha), bas barish dekh rahi thi.

Jai chahta tha kuch bole. "Barish tez hai na?" — itna simple sa sentence. Par muh se nikla kuch nahi.

Kya bolu? Kaise bolu? Wo sochegi main weird hu. Rehne de. Chup reh.

10 minute baad barish thodi kam hui. Aarohi chali gayi. Jai wahi khada raha — bheega hua, par barish ki wajah se nahi.

Homework ka ek aur din. Jai ne peeche se Aarohi ko "Homework kya hai?" pucha.

Bas. Ye thi unki baat. "Copy milegi?" "Homework kya hai?" "Thanks."

Chhoti cheezein. Par Jai ke liye — bohot badi cheezein.

Kyuki jab koi tumhe accha lagta hai, toh uski har "haan le lo" ek gift lagti hai. Aur uski har smile ek chapter.

Wo Letter

Saal khatam hone wala tha. March aa gaya tha. Exams paas the.

Aur Jai ke andar ek jung chal rahi thi — bol du ya nahi? Keh du ya chup rahu? Kya fayda bolne ka? Aur kya fayda chup rehne ka?

Agar nahi bola toh yahi regret rahega ki "kaash bol deta." Aur agar bola toh shayad reject ho jaunga. Par kam se kam try toh karunga.

Raat ko 11 baje Jai apne room me tha. Sab so rahe the. Wo ek plain page le ke baitha — pen haath me, par likhna nahi aa raha tha.

Pehla draft — filmy laga, faad diya.

Dusra draft — zyada formal laga, faad diya.

Teesra draft — bahut lambi ho gayi, faad diya.

Chautha draft — seedha, simple, honest:

"Aarohi, mujhe nahi pata ye sahi hai ya galat. Par main chup nahi reh sakta ab. Tum mujhe acchi lagti ho. Bahut. Kab se — shayad pehle din se. Main kuch expect nahi karta. Bas ye bolna tha."

Haath kaamp rahe the likhte waqt. Handwriting thodi kharab aayi. Par likh diya.

Letter fold kiya. Pocket me rakha. Aur poori raat soya nahi.

Agle din school. Jai ka dil itna tez dhadak raha tha ki lagta tha sab sun sakte hain.

Pura din wait kiya — sahi moment ka. Last period ke baad jab sab bahar ja rahe the, Jai ruka.

Aarohi apna bag pack kar rahi thi. Akeli thi — uski friend pehle nikal gayi thi.

Jai paas gaya. Haath me wo folded paper. Heart rate — 150 se upar.

Ab jo hoga dekha jayega.

Agle din. Jai school aaya — raat bhar soya nahi tha. Aankhon ke neeche halke circles. Dimaag me har possible scenario run kar chuka tha — "haan" se lekar "eww" tak. 47 scenarios. Literally count kiya tha.

First period. Aarohi apni jagah pe baithi thi. Normal. Koi tension nahi uske face pe. Jaise kuch hua hi nahi.

Jai wahi khada raha. Corridor me bacche jaa rahe the, shor tha, sab normal tha.

Par Jai ke liye duniya ek second ke liye ruk gayi thi. Phir wapas chalane lagi — par thodi dheemi.

Wo ready tha — aisa usne socha tha. Usne pehle se prepare kiya tha ki "nahi" sunega. Usne khud se bola tha ki "koi baat nahi, move on kar lunga."

Par sach me… wo ready nahi tha. Koi nahi hota.

Nayi Class, Purani Yaad

Us din ke baad sab same raha.

Class same. Log same. Routine same.

Aarohi ne kabhi us letter ka zikar nahi kiya — na doston ko bataya, na awkward behave kiya. Wo waise hi rahi jaise pehle thi — calm, quiet, apne kaam me busy. Jaise kuch hua hi nahi.

Aur shayad uske liye sach me kuch khaas nahi hua tha.

Par mere liye… sab badal gaya tha.

Bas ek cheez change hui.

Ab Jai uski taraf kam dekhta tha. Aur jab dekhta tha… toh jaldi nazar hata leta tha. Pehle dekhna aadat thi — ab dekhna dard tha.

Phir bhi — phir bhi — usne hope chhodi nahi. Andar kahin ek chhota sa hissa tha jo sochta tha:

9th me shayad sab better ho. Shayad ek naya start mile. Shayad wo bhi samjhe. Shayad time chahiye usse.

Wo wait karta raha.

Summer holidays aaye. Ghar pe tha — boring din, garam raatein. Phone pe kabhi kabhi school ka group dekhta tha. Aarohi kabhi online dikhi toh dil tez hota — par message nahi kiya. Kyuki kya likhta?

July aaya. Naya session. 9th class.

Nayi books. Nayi copies. Nayi class.

Aur… nayi sections.

Jai ne class me andar jaake sabse pehle ek kaam kiya — nazar ghumayi. Har face dekha. Har corner check kiya.

Aarohi nahi thi.

Wo ab dusri section me thi. Na wo seat thi. Na wo daily ek jhalak. Na wo dheemi awaaz jo teacher ke sawaal ka jawab deti thi. Na wo khidki ke bahar dekhti hui figure.

Sab ekdum se ruk gaya.

Jaise koi story shuru hone se pehle hi khatam ho gayi.

Kabhi kabhi corridor me dikhti thi — door se. Kabhi assembly me. Kabhi canteen ke paas.

Par ab wo "paas" nahi thi. Wo sirf ek aur face thi crowd me — wahi face jisko Jai ne 8th class ke pehle din "normal" samjha tha.

Circle complete ho gaya. Par feelings nahi.

Ab class me Jai ab bhi hasta hai. Doston ke saath mazaak karta hai. Naye log aaye, nayi baatein shuru hui. Sabko lagta hai sab normal hai.

Par kabhi kabhi —

Jab class thodi shaant hoti hai. Ya jab wo akela hota hai. Ya jab raat ko neend nahi aati.

Toh ek thought aa hi jaata hai:

"Kaash… thoda pehle bol deta."

Aur kabhi kabhi…

"Kaash… wo ek baar ruk jaati."
