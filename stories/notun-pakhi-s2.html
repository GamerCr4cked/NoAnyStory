Wapas

Saal beet gaya.

10th khatam hua. Boards aye, gaye, results aaye. Main pass ho gaya — koi topper nahi tha, par fail bhi nahi hua. Life move on kar gayi jaise sabki karti hai. Naye log, naye plans, naya chapter.

Par ek cheez move on nahi hui thi — wo shortcut wala rasta. Main aaj bhi kabhi kabhi usi se guzarta tha. Aadat thi. Ya shayad umeed.

College section same school me tha — sirf building alag, gate alag, par campus same. Toh technically main "school se gaya" nahi tha, bas upar shift ho gaya tha. 11th class — Science stream, Physics ke formulas aur Chemistry ke bonds. Zindagi boring ho gayi thi, honestly.

Ek August ki subah. Barish ruk gayi thi par hawa geeli thi. Main college building ke corridor me khada tha, phone dekhte hue, kuch nahi karte hue — jaise har din karta tha.

Tabhi neeche school building ki taraf nazar gayi. Gate se bacche aa rahe the — chhote chhote bags, loud awaazein, usual chaos.

Aur unhi ke beech se —

Ek familiar figure.

Pehle laga aankh dhoka de rahi hai. Main thoda aage jhuka. Squint kiya. Phir ek baar aur dekha.

Mahiza.

Wahi walk. Wahi bag ka style. Wahi expression — thoda serious, thoda lost in thought. Par kuch badla hua bhi tha — thodi badi lag rahi thi. 8th class ab. Aur baal thode chhote kar liye the.

Mera dil itna tez dhakka ki maine phone almost gira diya.

Wo wapas aa gayi?

Poora din dimaag me ek hi loop chala. Kaise? Kab? Family reason se gayi thi — toh kya family reason se wapas aayi? Ya koi aur school pasand nahi aaya? Ya — aur ye thought maine jaldi se dabaya — kya iss school me wapas aane ki koi aur wajah thi?

Nahi nahi. Itna mat soch. Tu college section me hai ab. Usse milna bhi practically mushkil hai.

Par school aur college ka lunch break same time hota tha. Canteen same thi. Ground same tha. Gate same tha.

Aur main same tha.

Teen din guzre bina kuch hue. Main roz neeche dekhta tha corridor se — kabhi dikhti, kabhi nahi. Par baat karne ka chance nahi mila. Ab main 10th wala baccha nahi tha — ab 11th ka "college student" tha. Neeche school section me jaana thoda odd lagta.

Chauthe din lunch break me main canteen ki taraf ja raha tha. Line lagi hui thi. Main apna phone scroll kar raha tha.

Tabhi ek awaaz aayi — itni naturally jaise rozana sunata ho:

Mahiza
line me toh lag jao, phone baad me dekh lena

Main phone se upar dekha. Wo mere side me khadi thi — hath me tiffin box, expression pe wo trademark halki si smile.

Ek second laga process karne me. Phir —

Mahiza

Tamim

Mahiza

Us din canteen me saath me khade rehe. Koi deep baat nahi hui — usne apna tiffin khola, mujhe ek paratha offer kiya (maine le liya), aur hum dono alag alag nikle.

Par ab ek cheez fix thi:

Season 2 shuru ho chuka tha.

Naye Din

Pehle hafte me hi ek routine ban gaya — bina kisi ne plan kiye.

Lunch break pe canteen. Main apni taraf se aata, wo apni taraf se. Kabhi line me mil jaate, kabhi seating area me. Koi fix time nahi tha, koi "milte hain" nahi bola tha — par roz mil jaate the. Jaise universe ne Google Calendar me event daal rakha ho.

Ek din wo apne friends ke saath baithi thi. Maine door se dekha aur socha aaj nahi disturb karta. Par usne mujhe dekh liya — aur haath hilaya. Seedha. Sabke saamne.

Main uski taraf dekha. Wo barish dekh rahi thi — par uske kaano pe halka sa laal rang tha. Barish ki wajah se ya kisi aur wajah se — ye main kabhi confirm nahi kar paaya.

Mere college ke dost puchte the — "wo kaun hai jo tujhse milti hai lunch pe?" Main bolta "junior hai, dost hai." Wo log smirk karte. Main ignore karta. Classic routine.

College Wala

Sports day tha. Ground pe shamiyaana laga tha, speakers pe commentary chal rahi thi, aur har taraf shor. Main apne college ke dosto ke saath stands me tha — technically "support" karne aaya tha, practically Mahiza ka match dekhne.

Bhai wo acchi thi.

Humara Normal

November aate aate sab kuch settle ho gaya tha. Na koi zyada sochna tha, na koi define karna tha. Hum dost the — par wo wale dost jinke beech ek unspoken understanding hoti hai.

Har din milte the. Har raat baat karte the. Aur har encounter me kuch naya pata chalta tha ek dusre ke baare me.

Mujhe pata chala:

  • Wo Rabindranath Tagore padhti hai — Bengali me, original me
  • Usse cats pasand hain par uski mummy ko allergy hai
  • Wo raat ko 2 baje tak jaag ke kuch likhti hai — diary ya stories, batati nahi
  • Uska favourite colour navy blue hai — par sab ko bolti hai "green"
  • Aur usse ye bilkul pasand nahi jab koi usse "chhoti" bole

Usse pata chala:

  • Main gaming karta hu — par usme bhi zyada creative mode wala hu, competitive nahi
  • Mujhe likhne ka shauk hai par kabhi kisi ko nahi dikhata
  • Main overthink karta hu — har cheez ke 4 scenarios sochta hu
  • Mujhe crowd se dar lagta hai par 2-3 logon me comfortable hu

Par ab ek cheez fix hai — jab tak hum dono usi campus me hain, usi canteen me, usi gate ke paas —

Ye story chalti rahegi.

Aur humara "kuch aur" —
