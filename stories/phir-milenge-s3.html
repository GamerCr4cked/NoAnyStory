2 Saal Baad

College khatam ho gaya.

Arhan ne degree li — BSc complete. Photography side me serious ho gayi thi. Freelance kaam aane laga tha. Ek chhote se studio me intern laga. Naye sheher me shift hua — akela, ek chhota sa kamra, ek camera, aur ek routine jo usne khud banaya tha.

Inaya ka bhi degree ho gaya. Usne content writing me kaam pakda — ek startup me. Dusre sheher me. Different life, different people, different everything.

Do saal ho gaye the — jab se garden wali raat. Jab se corridor me nod kiya tha. Jab se "phir milenge" ek vaada tha aur ab sirf ek yaad.

Arhan ab pehle wala Arhan nahi tha.

Wo better tha — kuch maayno me. Photography ne usse ek awaaz di. Jo shabd nahi bol paata tha, wo images se kehta tha. Uski Instagram pe ek series thi — "Khamoshi Ke Rang" — jisme wo empty benches, abandoned corridors, akeli sadakein click karta tha. Logon ko pasand aayi. Kisi ko pata nahi tha ki har photo ke peeche ek specific yaad thi.

Par wo emotionally band tha. Vikram se baat karta tha — college ke baad bhi wo dost raha — kaam pe professional tha, par kisi ko paas nahi aane deta tha.

Ek boundary thi jo usne apne around banayi thi — transparent, par titanium jaisi strong.

Kuch log tootne ke baad jud jaate hain. Kuch log tootne ke baad aur mazboot ho jaate hain. Par kuch log tootne ke baad — sirf ek nayi shape me dhal jaate hain. Wo same nahi rehte. Na kamzor, na mazboot. Bas… alag.

Inaya bhi badal gayi thi.

Wo pehle wali carefree Inaya nahi thi. Wo jo green tea 2 sip me chhod deti thi, jo baarish me bheegne kheench ke le jaati thi, jo har baat pe comeback rakhti thi — wo ab thodi quiet thi. Thodi measured. Jaise har shabd sochke bolti thi — kyuki ek baar bina sochke kiya tha aur sab barbad ho gaya tha.

Wo Rehan se kabhi baat nahi ki uske baad. Block tha — aur block raha.

Uski diary me — jo college me shuru ki thi — aakhri entry 2 saal purani thi:

"Maine apni zindagi ka sabse accha insaan khoya. Meri galti. Poori tarah meri galti. Aur sabse bura ye hai ki — main usse sorry bhi nahi bol paayi properly. Kyuki sorry se kuch nahi hota jab trust toot jaata hai."

Rebuild

Phir Milenge

Waqt liya. Arhan ne waqt liya.

Ek mahina aur guzra. Calls hoti rahi. Texts aate rahe. Par ab ek difference tha — Inaya poori tarah transparent thi. Koi chhupa nahi rahi thi. Koi "chill karo" nahi. Koi jhooth nahi. Har chhoti baat — batati thi. Kahaan hai, kiske saath hai, kya kar rahi hai. Bina Arhan ke puche.

Ye trust rebuild karna tha — brick by brick, day by day.

Phir Milenge

Friendship. Love. Betrayal. Heartbreak. Silence. Healing. Aur phir — ek nayi shuruaat.


Kuch log tootke bikhar jaate hain.

Kuch log tootke jud jaate hain.

Aur kuch log tootke — ek naya shape le lete hain. Saath me.
