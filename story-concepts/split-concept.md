# Split — Story Concept (SAVED FOR LATER)

## Genre: Roommate Romance / Heartbreak / Betrayal
## Language: Hinglish
## Chapters: 14

## Concept
Low budget ki wajah se ladka-ladki same flat share karte hain. Dono same college class ke hain. Enemies to lovers journey — par twist ye hai ki ladki manipulative hai aur ladke ko use karti hai (rent, expenses, favours). Audience ko pata nahi chalta kyuki 99% POV ladke ka hai. Jab reveal hota hai toh audience ko bhi wahi dhoka lagta hai jo ladke ko laga.

## Characters
- **Ladka** — Simple, middle class, side business (freelancing). Genuine feelings develop hoti hain.
- **Ladki** — Charming, smart, par manipulative. Umeed alive rakhti hai par kabhi clearly haan nahi bolti.
- **"College Friend"** — Actually ladki ka boyfriend. Ch10 me introduce hota hai.

## Key Writing Trick
Ch3-9 me ladki ke dialogues double meaning wale — genuine bhi lagein, baad me manipulative bhi samajh aayein. Audience ladke ke saath dhoka khaye.
